# Weekly Prayer Points: Faithful Stewardship
*Based on: "What Will You Do With What God Gave You?" by Bishop Darlingston Johnson*

## Focus Areas

### 1. Prioritizing the Gospel
**Scripture**: 1 Thessalonians 2:4  
- Pray that we will prioritize the gospel in all aspects of our lives, understanding that we are entrusted with the message of salvation.  
- Ask God to help us make the gospel our first priority in our time, talents, and treasure.  

### 2. Personalizing the Mission
**Scripture**: Matthew 28:19-20  
- Pray for the conviction to take personal responsibility for the Great Commission, recognizing it as our individual calling.  
- Ask the Holy Spirit to reveal specific ways we can share the gospel in our unique spheres of influence.  

### 3. Participating Fully in God’s Work
**Scripture**: John 9:4  
- Pray for grace to work faithfully and diligently in spreading the gospel while we have the opportunity.  
- Ask God to help us avoid complacency and commit to full-time faithfulness in using our resources for His kingdom.  

### 4. Faithfulness in Stewardship
**Scripture**: Matthew 25:21  
- Pray for a heart that seeks to be a good and faithful servant, using all that God has given us for His glory.  
- Ask God to help us be trustworthy in managing our time, talents, and treasure, no matter how much or little we have.  

### 5. Overcoming Procrastination
**Scripture**: Proverbs 6:6-8  
- Pray for deliverance from procrastination and fear that hinders us from acting on God’s call immediately.  
- Ask for courage and wisdom to take decisive steps in fulfilling God’s purposes without delay.  

### 6. Embracing an Eternal Perspective
**Scripture**: 2 Corinthians 4:18  
- Pray that we will focus on eternal rewards rather than earthly distractions, living daily with heaven in mind.  
- Ask God to keep us motivated by the reality of standing before Him to give an account of our stewardship.  

### 7. Active vs. Passive Faith
**Scripture**: James 2:17  
- Pray that we will be active believers, fully engaged in kingdom work and not merely consumers of God’s blessings.  
- Ask God to give us a spirit of boldness to invest our lives in ways that bear fruit for eternity.  

### 8. Trusting God’s Measure
**Scripture**: Luke 16:10  
- Pray for contentment and trust in God’s measure, knowing He values our faithfulness over the quantity of our resources.  
- Ask God to show us how to maximize what we have been given for His glory.  

## Intercession
- Pray for the church community to unite in prioritizing the gospel and fulfilling the Great Commission.  
- Lift up leaders, pastors, and ministry workers, asking God to strengthen them to lead by example in faithful stewardship.  
- Pray for families to commit their time, talents, and treasure to God, raising the next generation to live for His kingdom.  

## Declarations
- "I declare that I am a faithful steward of the time, talents, and treasure God has entrusted to me. I will prioritize the gospel in all I do."  
- "I declare that I will not bury what God has given me but will fully invest in His kingdom for His glory."  

## Closing Prayer
Heavenly Father, we thank You for entrusting us with Your gospel and the resources to grow Your kingdom. Help us to prioritize Your message above all else, personalize the mission as our own responsibility, and participate fully in proclaiming the gospel. Deliver us from procrastination, fear, and complacency. Teach us to be faithful stewards of what You have given us, whether much or little, and to live with an eternal perspective. May we hear the words, “Well done, good and faithful servant,” when we stand before You. Strengthen Your church, empower our leaders, and unite families in this mission. We declare that we will be active believers, investing in Your kingdom daily. In Jesus’ name, amen.

## Big Idea
God has entrusted each of us with resources—time, talents, and treasure—to proclaim the gospel, make disciples, and grow His kingdom. Faithfulness to this call determines our eternal reward and impact for His glory.

## Outline
### 1. Prioritize the Message
- The gospel must be first in our time, talents, and treasure.
- It is the power of God for salvation and must not be delayed or minimized.

### 2. Personalize the Mission
- The Great Commission is not just for pastors or evangelists but for every believer.
- Each person must take personal responsibility to spread the gospel where they are.

### 3. Participate Fully
- God is not looking for part-time missionaries; He calls us to full-time faithfulness in every season.
- We must consistently use our resources daily for the kingdom, not sporadically or out of convenience.

### 4. Faithfulness is Essential
- The parable of the talents (Matthew 25:14-30) underscores the importance of using what God has entrusted for His purposes.
- Faithful servants receive greater responsibility and eternal reward; unfaithful servants suffer loss.

### 5. Two Types of Believers
- Active believers: Those who work, contribute, and invest in the kingdom.
- Passive believers: Those who consume, procrastinate, and bury their God-given resources.
- Procrastination is "disobedience in slow motion."

### 6. God's Measure of Faithfulness
- God values what we do with what we have, not how much we have.
- Whether much or little, our resources should be used to proclaim the gospel, make disciples, and grow His kingdom.

### 7. Eternal Perspective
- Earthly resources and achievements are small compared to the eternal rewards awaiting faithful stewardship.
- Live daily with the reality of giving an account to Jesus, striving for His commendation: "Well done, good and faithful servant."

## Applications
1. Reflect on how you are prioritizing the gospel in your daily life. Are you dedicating your time, talents, and treasure to kingdom work?
2. Take personal responsibility for the Great Commission. Identify specific ways you can participate in proclaiming the gospel where you are.
3. Avoid procrastination. Start using your resources immediately to glorify God and grow His kingdom.

## Prayer Points
1. Pray for a heart that prioritizes the gospel and faithfully uses God's resources for His glory.
2. Ask God to deliver you from procrastination and give you the courage to act immediately in obedience.
3. Seek the Holy Spirit's guidance in identifying specific ways to invest your time, talents, and treasure for the kingdom.

## Call to Respond
Evaluate your stewardship before God. Are you prioritizing, personalizing, and fully participating in the mission He has given you? Commit to faithfulness today so that when you stand before Jesus, you will hear, "Well done, good and faithful servant." Let there be no regrets—live for His eternal approval, not earthly applause.

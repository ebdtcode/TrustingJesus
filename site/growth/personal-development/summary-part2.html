Core Theme

The Central Concept

A life plan is about moving you from where you are (HERE) to where you ought to be (THERE).

From 2024 to 2034 - from here to there. A lot can happen in 10 years.

Paul's Mindset: The Upward Call

Philippians 3:12-14

Principle 1

God has a place He wants me to be.

God has a plan for my life. There is a destination God has prepared.

Principle 2

I must do everything I can to get there.

Press on, forget what's behind, reach forward to what lies ahead.

Upward vs. Forward

  • Forward = Moving on the same level (difficult but manageable)
  • Upward = Moving against gravity (requires extra push)

"What God has for me is not just forward - it's upward. I have to push extra hard to get to where God wants me to be."

HERE vs. THERE

HERE = Your Present

  • Where you are right now
  • Could be good, bad, or mixed
  • You cannot stay here forever
  • Time is always moving

THERE = Your Future

  • Where God wants you to be
  • Could be physical, spiritual, or mental
  • Requires intentional movement
  • Must be clearly defined

What Got You Here?

External Factors

Things done to you or for you:

  • Country you were born in
  • Parents you didn't choose
  • Circumstances beyond your control

Internal Factors

Things you did for yourself or to yourself:

  • Choices you made
  • Actions you took
  • Patterns you reinforced or broke

"Your life is not an accident, and nobody's life is an accident. There's always a logic behind everybody's life."

The Four Steps from Elijah (1 Kings 17)

1. GET AWAY

Make a Move

  • You can't move by staying
  • Take a step (even small)
  • Talk, register, seek advice
"One small step for a man, one giant leap for mankind."

2. TURN

Change Direction

  • You end up where you're headed
  • Break generational patterns
  • Paddle against the current

What people call "generational curses" are often just repeated choices.

3. HIDE

Set Yourself Apart

  • Important things are done alone
  • Public things are often for show
  • Real change happens privately

If you listen to everyone, you'll never do anything.

4. DRINK

Receive Fresh Input

Get something fresh in your life: new knowledge, new information, new relationships, new concepts. Expose yourself to what you haven't known before.

Power Quotes

"A lot can happen in 10 years. Don't take for granted what God can accomplish with your life and through your life."

"Time requires that you do not let only things happen to you. You must plan for things before they happen to you."

"I don't want the next 10 years to shock any one of us. Please, be a fool now. Not there. Take all the right steps now."

Application Questions

For Self-Assessment

  1. What is your "here"? (Honestly assess your current state)
  2. What external factors brought you here?
  3. What internal choices brought you here?
  4. What is your "there"? (Where does God want you to be?)
  5. What forces are pushing against you getting there?

The Four Steps Applied

  1. GET AWAY: What first step do you need to take?
  2. TURN: What direction do you need to change?
  3. HIDE: What do you need to do privately, away from public opinion?
  4. DRINK: What new knowledge or relationships do you need?

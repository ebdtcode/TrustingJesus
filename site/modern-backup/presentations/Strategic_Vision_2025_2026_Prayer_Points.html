ðŸ”¥ Prayer Point 1

Divine Expansion for the Second Service

Focus: Pray that God enlarges our capacity to accommodate the growth He is bringing to Harvest Intercontinental Church. Ask for wisdom in planning, anointed leadership, and supernatural provision for the second service launching on April 5, 2026.

"Enlarge the place of your tent, stretch your tent curtains wide, do not hold back; lengthen your cords, strengthen your stakes."
Isaiah 54:2-3 (NIV)

Declaration: "We declare that our tent is enlarging! God is making room for the multitudes He is sending to Harvest Church. The second service will be filled with hungry souls ready to encounter the living God!"

ðŸ”¥ Prayer Point 2

Spirit-Led Worship & Anointed Teaching

Focus: Pray for the anointing of the Holy Spirit to rest upon every aspect of the second service - worship, teaching, ministry, and fellowship. Ask that unity and the presence of God flow like precious oil over both services.

"How good and pleasant it is when God's people live together in unity! It is like precious oil poured on the head, running down on the beard, running down on Aaron's beard, down on the collar of his robe. For there the Lord bestows his blessing, even life forevermore."
Psalm 133:1-3 (NIV)

Declaration: "We declare that the oil of God's anointing flows upon both services at Harvest Church! Where there is unity, God commands His blessing. The second service will be a place of powerful worship and life-transforming teaching!"

ðŸ”¥ Prayer Point 3

Emerging Leaders Initiative - Raised Up & Ready

Focus: Pray that young leaders between ages 25-45 will answer God's call to step into leadership roles. Ask for boldness, wisdom, and a servant's heart as they take their places in ministry and service.

"Don't let anyone look down on you because you are young, but set an example for the believers in speech, in conduct, in love, in faith and in purity."
1 Timothy 4:12 (NIV)

Declaration: "We declare that this generation of leaders will not be despised for their youth! They are examples in word, conduct, love, faith, and purity. God is raising them up NOW to lead with power and integrity!"

Bold Leaders - "Here Am I, Send Me!"

Focus: Pray for the Emerging Leaders Initiative to produce leaders who eagerly respond to God's call with Isaiah's famous cry: "Here am I, send me!" Ask for supernatural equipping through the Holy Spirit to fulfill their assignments.

"Then I heard the voice of the Lord saying, 'Whom shall I send? And who will go for us?' And I said, 'Here am I. Send me!'"
Isaiah 6:8 (NIV)

Declaration: "We declare that emerging leaders at Harvest Church are answering the call! With boldness they cry, 'Here am I, send me!' They are equipped, anointed, and ready to serve in this new season!"

ðŸ”¥ Prayer Point 5

5 Nights of Fire - Shekinah Glory Manifestation

Focus: Pray for the 5 Nights of Fire (December 1-23, 2025) to be marked by unprecedented manifestations of God's glory. Ask that His presence fills the earth and our gatherings as the waters cover the sea - completely, fully, undeniably!

"For the earth will be filled with the knowledge of the glory of the Lord as the waters cover the sea."
Habakkuk 2:14 (NIV)

Declaration: "We declare that the Shekinah glory of the Lord will manifest in a way we have never seen before! The 5 Nights of Fire will be saturated with His presence. Men and women will hunger for His glory more than they crave for bread!"

ðŸ”¥ Prayer Point 6

Tsunami of Souls - Unusual Miracles, Signs & Wonders

Focus: Pray for a tsunami of souls to flood into the Kingdom during the 5 Nights of Fire and beyond. Ask for unusual miracles, signs, and wonders that will authenticate the Gospel and draw multitudes to Christ through Watchnight and into 2026.

"In the last days, God says, I will pour out my Spirit on all people. Your sons and daughters will prophesy, your young men will see visions, your old men will dream dreams. Even on my servants, both men and women, I will pour out my Spirit in those days, and they will prophesy. I will show wonders in the heavens above and signs on the earth below."
Acts 2:17-18 (NIV)

Declaration: "We declare a tsunami of souls is coming to Harvest Church! The Lord is pouring out His Spirit. We will see unusual miracles, signs, and wonders that confirm His Word. Multitudes will be saved. Let it manifest in an unprecedented way!"

The Gift of Eternal Life

"And this is the testimony: God has given us eternal life, and this life is in His Son."

1 John 5:11

  • Eternal life is not just a future promise
  • It is God's very life, nature, and ability
  • Imparted to your spirit when you were born again

Divine Energy Within

"What gasoline does for a car, what jet fuel does for an airplane, what blood does for the body - eternal life does for your spirit."

  • This life energizes your spirit to function
  • Enables you to walk in love
  • Conforms you to Christ's image

You Have the Capacity

"We know that we have passed from death to life, because we love the brothers."

1 John 3:14

  • The love of God is already in your heart
  • Shed abroad through the Holy Spirit
  • The capacity to love as Christ loves is within you

God's Extreme Love for You

"As the Father has loved me, I also have loved you; abide in my love."

John 15:9

God loves you with the same intensity that He loves Jesus Christ.

The Cold Apartment

  • Story: Newly married couple freezing in their apartment
  • Bundled in blankets, thought heat was external
  • Didn't know the thermostat was on the wall
  • Heat was available all along!

"If you are not aware of the love of God, you're going to end up freezing unnecessarily."

The Four Keys

To Walking in God's Love Daily

  • 1. AWARENESS
  • 2. ACKNOWLEDGMENT
  • 3. ABIDING
  • 4. ACTION

Key #1: AWARENESS

"I see how much God loves me, and I continue to grow every day in my awareness of that love."

  • Develop deep, personal revelation of God's love
  • Ignorance hinders our ability to walk in love
  • This awareness must continually increase

How to Grow in Awareness

"That you may know the love of Christ which surpasses knowledge."

Ephesians 3:19

  • Pray daily for increased revelation
  • Pray in the Spirit (Jude 1:20-21)
  • Meditate on scriptures about God's love
  • Focus on John 15:9, 16:27, and 3:16

Key #2: ACKNOWLEDGMENT

"I agree with and confess what God says about His love for all of His children, including me."

"That the sharing of your faith may become effective by the acknowledgment of every good thing which is in you in Christ Jesus."

Philemon 1:6

Flip the Switch

"What you agree with, you activate."

  • Confession releases what grace has already provided
  • Your words flip the switch that releases God's love
  • Vocal acknowledgment gives your spirit permission
  • Declare God's love even when feelings disagree

Key #3: ABIDING

"I rely on His love for me. I rely on His love in me for everything."

"Abide in my love."

John 15:9

Abiding in Practice

"Jesus never told us to produce love. He told us to simply abide in love."

  • Make God's love your permanent dwelling place
  • Refuse to doubt regardless of circumstances
  • Rely totally on God's love for all answers
  • Return to the source when empty, without condemnation

Daily Declaration

Each Morning Say:

"Father, thank You that You love me the same way You love Jesus. I receive and rely on this love today."

"You don't love others FOR God. You love others THROUGH God."

Key #4: ACTION

"I allow His love to flow freely through me to others. I allow Christ to love through me."

"This is my commandment, that you love one another as I have loved you."

John 15:12

How Love Flows

  • Not forced, but freely flowing from abiding
  • Manifests as patience, kindness, mercy
  • Expressed through forgiveness and generosity
  • Makes God visible to the world

"Every time you love, you're making God Himself more and more visible to the world."

Forgiveness Through Love

  • When struggling to forgive, abide in this revelation:

"This person is loved by God as much as He loves Jesus."

  • Meditate on God's love for them
  • Let His love melt your resistance
  • Flow pattern: Freely receive, freely give

Prayer Points: Awareness

  • For Revelation: Open my eyes to see and understand the depth of Your love
  • For Daily Growth: Let my awareness of Your love increase every day
  • For the Church: Pour out corporate revelation of Your unfailing love

Prayer Points: Acknowledgment

  • For Bold Confession: Help me declare Your love over my life daily
  • For Overcoming Doubt: Let my confession stand firm when feelings disagree
  • For Activation: As I agree with Your Word, activate Your love in me

Prayer Points: Abiding

  • For Total Reliance: Teach me to depend completely on Your love
  • For Difficult People: Love them through me as I abide in You
  • For Praying in Spirit: Keep me anchored in Your love through prayer

Prayer Points: Action

  • For Forgiveness: Let Your love flow through me to release others
  • For Practical Love: Manifest Your love in my daily actions
  • For Witness: Make God visible through my love for others

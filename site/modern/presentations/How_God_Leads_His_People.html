3. Through Prayer and Seeking God's Will

"in all your ways submit to him, and he will make your paths straight."
Proverbs 3:6 (NIV)

Prayer opens the way for God to give guidance through peace, scripture, or circumstances.

üôè Prayer Aligns Our Hearts

  • Trust in the LORD with all your heart
  • Lean not on your own understanding
  • Submit to Him in all your ways
  • He will make your paths straight

Example: David often sought God's counsel before making decisions in battle (1 Samuel 23:2, 30:8).

4. Through Godly Counsel and Fellowship

"Plans fail for lack of counsel, but with many advisers they succeed."
Proverbs 15:22 (NIV)

God sometimes leads us through the wisdom of mature believers.

üë• The Value of Wise Advisers

  • Many advisers help plans succeed
  • Seek godly counsel before major decisions

Example: Paul and Barnabas were commissioned for ministry after prayer and counsel from the church in Antioch (Acts 13:2-3).

Key Truths to Remember

  • Scripture is our primary and most reliable source of guidance
  • The Holy Spirit actively guides believers into truth
  • Through prayer, God aligns our hearts with His purposes
  • Godly advisers help confirm what God is speaking
  • God works through circumstances, both favorable and challenging
  • Inner peace is often a sign of God's will
  • God's guidance often unfolds gradually; trust the process

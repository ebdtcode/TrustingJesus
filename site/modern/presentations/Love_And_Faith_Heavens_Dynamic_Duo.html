The Power of God's Love

The Strongest Force in the Universe

God's Love: The Strongest Force

  • God's love is not passive sentiment - it's the greatest power in the universe
  • If God is for you, who can be against you? (Romans 8:31)
  • Love never fails - it is as strong as God Himself (1 Corinthians 13)
  • Everything else can fail - knowledge, people, money - but God's love cannot

"Love never fails."

1 Corinthians 13:8

Love is God in Action

  • Love is not passive - love is action
  • When God says "I love you," He means: "I am acting on your behalf"
  • God's love = God eternally committed to using His power for your good
  • Right now, God is using His almighty power in your interest

Nothing Can Overcome God's Love

No obstacle, no problem, no challenge is strong enough to prevent God's love from accomplishing His will for you!

The Dynamic Duo

How Love and Faith Work Together

Love Provides, Faith Receives

God's Love

  • The power that provides
  • Already supplied all we need
  • Constantly flowing in abundance
  • Never takes a vacation

Our Faith

  • The mechanism that receives
  • Brings us into alignment
  • Releases love's provision
  • Is actually a rest, not striving
"Faith is not hard. Faith is actually a rest - resting in the love of God."

Faith Aligns Us With Love

  • Faith doesn't make God do anything - love has already caused God to do everything
  • In Christ, we are complete - God supplied all our needs (Philippians 4:19)
  • Faith is how we get into alignment with what love has already done
  • Like putting your cup under water that's already flowing

"For God so loved the world that He gave His only begotten Son..."

John 3:16

Activating Faith

Two Keys to Release God's Power

Key 1: Walk in Love

  • Faith works by love (Galatians 5:6)
  • When we walk in love, we activate faith
  • Love gives, serves, and sacrifices - when you do this, you align with God's love
  • "I will show you my faith by my works" - works of love (James 2:18)
"When I choose to walk in love - giving, serving, sacrificing - I'm activating the power of faith that allows me to receive and release God's love."

Key 2: Open Your Mouth

  • The spirit of faith: "We believe, therefore we speak" (2 Corinthians 4:13)
  • With the heart one believes, with the mouth confession is made (Romans 10:10)
  • Jesus said: "Say to this mountain, be removed" - speaking is spiritual law
  • Your mouth was given primarily to activate the covenant, not just to eat!

"If you confess with your mouth the Lord Jesus and believe in your heart that God has raised Him from the dead, you will be saved."

Romans 10:9

Put Your Mouth Where Love Flows

  • Love is flowing in Jesus - put your mouth where Jesus is!
  • Declare who Jesus is, what He has done, who you are in Him
  • Declare: He's my righteousness, my supply, my healing, my protection
  • Get your mouth off your problems and onto God's promises!

A Silent Christian is a Weak Christian

Not because power is unavailable - but because silence neutralizes our receiving!

The Power of Corporate Agreement

  • If two agree concerning anything, it shall be done (Matthew 18:19)
  • Imagine a whole church vocalizing agreement with God's Word!
  • Corporate vocal prayer creates a legal environment for heaven to move
  • This is what terrifies the devil - not our silence, but our unified declaration
"When we vocalize our agreement, we create a legal environment that allows heaven to work powerfully and forces the enemy to flee."

Based on 2 Corinthians 4:13; Romans 10:8-10

The 25th Bishopric Celebration

Prayer Points

April 2-6, 2025

Celebrating 25 Years of Faithful Leadership

Give Thanks to the Lord

"It is good to give thanks to the LORD, and to sing praises to Your name, O Most High; to declare Your lovingkindness in the morning, and Your faithfulness every night."

Psalm 92:1-2

  • Thank God, praise his Holy Name as HIM-U celebrates 25 years of the Bishopric
  • Thank God for His consistent lovingkindness and faithfulness to Bishop and Lady Chrys
  • Declare that HIM-U will fulfill her mission of winning the lost at all costs

Shepherds After God's Heart

"And I will give you shepherds according to My heart, who will feed you with knowledge and understanding."

Jeremiah 3:15

"The eyes of your understanding being enlightened; that you may know what is the hope of His calling, what are the riches of the glory of His inheritance in the saints."

Ephesians 1:18

Prayer for Bishop and Lady Chrys

  • Thank God for Bishop and Lady Chrys who obeyed the Lord to become missionaries and not refugees
  • Thank God for their faithful, committed shepherding of HIM-U
  • Pray that God continues to enlighten their eyes of understanding in their knowledge of Him spiritually and experientially
  • Pray that the Spirit of the Lord will rest on them mightily as they lead HIM-U
  • Pray they lack nothing good spiritually, emotionally, physically, and financially

Lifting Up the Hands

"But Moses' hands became heavy; so they took a stone and put it under him, and he sat on it. And Aaron and Hur supported his hands, one on one side, and the other on the other side; and his hands were steady until the going down of the sun. So Joshua defeated Amalek and his people with the edge of the sword."

Exodus 17:12-13

Prayer for All HIM-U Bishops

  • Thank God for Bishop Musa Korfeh, Bishop Bouerima Kimso, Bishop Julius Lagaah, Bishop Daniel Njoya, Bishop Victor Okofor, Bishop Wolo Belleh, and their First Ladies
  • Thank God for those who are lifting up the hands of Bishop Johnson and Lady Chrys
  • Pray that they continuously trust in the Lord and lean on His understanding as they serve HIM-U
  • Declare that they prosper and be in good health even as their souls prosper

Provision for All Needs

"And my God shall supply all your need according to His riches in glory by Christ Jesus."

Philippians 4:19

Safe Travel and Provision

  • Agree with the Word of God that those needing Visas will receive them
  • None will lack travel and accommodation finances whether they travel internationally or locally
  • Declare all modes of transportation are covered by the blood of Jesus
  • Cancel incidents and accidents as people come and go back to their respective destinations

Volunteers and Committees

"Above all, clothe yourselves with love, which binds us all together in perfect harmony. And let the peace that comes from Christ rule in your hearts. For as members of one body you are called to live in peace. And always be thankful."

Colossians 3:14-15 (NLT)

Prayer for Committee Workers

  • Thank God for the people who have volunteered to work in various Committees to organize The Bishopric
  • Pray that brotherly love binds them and peace prevails from beginning to end
  • Pray for wisdom, counsel, strength, tenacity, and joy as they serve the Lord this way

Ministry Workers of Harvest Olney

"Work willingly at whatever you do, as though you were working for the Lord rather than for people. Remember that the Lord will give you an inheritance as your reward, and that the Master you are serving is Christ."

Colossians 3:23-24 (NLT)

Serving with Excellence

  • Thank God for all the Ministry Workers of Harvest Olney as they give themselves to serve and go the extra mile during The Bishopric
  • Pray for unity of purpose and of mind, peace and strength
  • Thank God for rewarding them in this life and the one to come

Financial Provision

"And God is able to make all grace abound toward you, that you, always having all sufficiency in all things, may have an abundance for every good work."

2 Corinthians 9:8

  • Declare that we have enough finances for all administrative needs and the Dinner
  • Pray for God's wisdom and strategies for the Finance Committee and they execute the plans

Demonstration of the Spirit's Power

"And my speech and my preaching were not with persuasive words of human wisdom, but in demonstration of the Spirit and of power, that your faith should not be in the wisdom of men but in the power of God."

1 Corinthians 2:4-5

Manifested Presence of God

  • Pray for the manifested presence of God during the evening services and teaching sessions during the day
  • Pray that the Word will be preached in the demonstration of the Spirit and of power
  • Pray a glory cloud to tabernacle with us during Praise and Worship Sessions

Confidence in Prayer

"Now this is the confidence that we have in Him, that if we ask anything according to His will, He hears us. And if we know that He hears us, whatever we ask, we know that we have the petitions that we have asked of Him."

1 John 5:14-15

  • Thank God that He has heard and answered our prayers

Day 9 - January 17th

Church Growth & Souls

"Constantly praising God and being in favor and goodwill with all the people; and the Lord kept adding [to their number] daily those who were being saved [from spiritual death]."

Acts 2:47 (AMPC)

  • Pray for numerical growth in Harvest Olney and HIMU

Open Doors for the Gospel

"For a great and effective door has opened to me, and there are many adversaries."

1 Corinthians 16:9 (NKJV)

  • Thank God for the souls in Olney that are coming to the Kingdom via Harvest Olney
  • Pray that God will give us strategies to win Olney/Greater Silver Spring for Him
  • Pray against adversaries occupying the gates of Olney/Greater Silver Spring and not allowing the people to receive Jesus Christ in their lives

A Resting Place

"He makes me lie down in green pastures. He leads me beside quiet waters."

Psalm 23:2 (NIV)

  • Pray that the Word preached and the worship in Harvest Olney will cause those visiting and regular attendees to find a resting place
  • Pray they will be established in the faith and decide to become members

The Great Work Continues

"I'm doing a great work; I can't come down. Why should the work come to a standstill just so I can come down to see you?"

Nehemiah 6:3 (MSG)

  • Thank God for the great work that He is doing through us in building His Sanctuary
  • Pray that despite the internal or external pressures, this great work will not stop until completed

Do Not Grow Weary

"Let us not become weary in doing good, for at the proper time we will reap a harvest if we do not give up."

Galatians 6:9 (NIV)

  • Pray that Harvesters will not be weary (fatigued/reluctant to) in giving towards Invest Beyond

The ELI Generation

"So, the priests and Levites consecrated themselves to bring up the Chest of the God of Israel. The Levites carried the Chest of God exactly as Moses, instructed by God, commanded."

1 Chronicles 15:15 (MSG)

  • Pray that the ELI generation will consecrate themselves for the work of God
  • Pray that the ELI generation will be obedient to the instructions of God through the church leadership
  • Pray that the ELI generation will take the work that God is entrusting them seriously

Seeking God for Themselves

"And so the Lord used to speak to Moses face to face, just as a man speaks to his friend. When Moses returned to the camp, his attendant Joshua, the son of Nun, a young man, would not depart from the tent."

Exodus 33:11 (AMP)

  • Pray that the ELI generation will not just serve but that they will be people who seek God for themselves

God's Faithfulness to HIMU

"The Lord's acts of mercy indeed do not end, For His compassions do not fail. They are new every morning; Great is Your faithfulness."

Lamentations 3:22-23 (NASB)

  • Thank God for His faithfulness towards HIMU
  • Declare that His act of mercy indeed will not end and His compassions will not fail

Prayer for Leadership

"Dear brothers and sisters, pray for us."

1 Thessalonians 5:25 (NLT)

  • Pray that Bishop Johnson and Lady Chrys will continue walking in divine health, the wisdom of God, divine protection, joy, strength and the anointing of the Lord
  • Pray for all Diocesan Bishops, that God give them wisdom and strength as they lead HIM-U
  • Pray for God's continuous protection, provision, and leading upon all HIM-U Pastors and Ministers and their spouses, and all missionaries

The Message Keeps Growing

"And the message of God kept on growing and spreading, and the number of disciples continued to increase greatly in Jerusalem; and a large number of the priests were becoming obedient to the faith."

Acts 6:7 (AMP)

  • Declare that the message of God will keep growing and spreading in all countries and cities where HIM-U is established
  • Declare that number of souls coming to Christ and disciples continue to increase greatly in all areas where Harvest Churches have been planted

Day 10 - January 18th

Open Doors & Unity

Colossians 4:3-4

  • Pray that the Lord will open more doors to HIM-U to plant vibrant churches and raise up disciples in the nations
  • Pray that the entire HIM-U will more fully embrace its apostolic mandate and its spiritual DNA to win the lost at costs and make disciples

"No weapon formed against you shall prosper, and every tongue which rises against you in judgment You shall condemn. This is the heritage of the servants of the Lord, and their righteousness is from Me," says the Lord.

Isaiah 54:17 (NKJV)

  • Declare that HIM-U goes from faith to faith, from strength to strength, and from glory to glory through Christ Jesus

Angelic Protection

Psalm 91:11

"The angel of the LORD encamps around those who fear Him, and He delivers them."

Psalm 34:7

  • Declare that the angels of the Lord encamp around all conference participants and deliver them
  • Declare that all delegates are protected in all their ways

Walk in Unity

"I appeal to you, dear brothers and sisters, by the authority of our Lord Jesus Christ, to live in harmony with each other. Let there be no divisions in the church. Rather, be of one mind, united in thought and purpose."

1 Corinthians 1:10 (NLT)

"Love one another with brotherly affection. Outdo one another in showing honor and sincere love for each other."

Romans 12:10 (ESV)

  • Declare that Harvesters will always walk in unity, being one mind, and united in thought and purpose
  • Pray against any form of division in the church

Led in Triumph

"But thanks be to God, who in Christ always leads us in triumphal procession, and through us spreads the fragrance of the knowledge of him everywhere."

2 Corinthians 2:14 (ESV)

  • Thank God for causing Harvesters to prevail and triumph
  • Pray that He will use us to spread the fragrance of His knowledge everywhere
  • Declare that regardless of the challenges we face, we expect a breakthrough, for if God is for us, who can possibly be against us?

Power to Be Witnesses

"But you shall receive power after that the Holy Ghost is come upon you: and you shall be witnesses unto me both in Jerusalem, and in all Judea, and in Samaria, and unto the uttermost part of the earth."

Acts 1:8

  • Pray for evangelistic grace upon Harvesters
  • Pray for souls in our Jerusalem and to the uttermost parts of the earth

Demonstration of Power

"And my preaching was not with enticing words of man's wisdom, but in demonstration of the Spirit and of power."

1 Corinthians 2:4

  • Pray that Harvest will continue to preach the unadulterated Word of God
  • Pray that miracles, signs wonders, and the manifested Presence of God will be evident in our gatherings

United in Vision

"Now I beseech you, brethren, by the Name of our Lord Jesus Christ, that you all speak the same thing, and that there be no divisions among you; but that you be perfectly joined together in the same mind and in the same judgment."

1 Corinthians 1:10

  • Pray that HIM-U will remain focused and united on the vision of a world where no one lives or dies without Christ
  • Pray that HIM-U and individual congregations will walk in unity of spirit and of mind

Prayer and the Word

"But we will give ourselves continually to prayer and the ministry of the Word."

Acts 6:4

  • Pray for a fresh hunger and thirst for prayer and for numerical increase in prayer meetings
  • Pray that Harvesters will make spending time in the Word a priority

Prayer for Government

"And seek the peace of the city whither I have caused you to be carried away captives, and pray unto God for it: For in the peace thereof shall you have peace."

Jeremiah 29:7

  • Pray that God will give wisdom to President Trump and all government leaders
  • Pray for peace to reign over chaos, and for godly leaders

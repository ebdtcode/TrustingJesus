Prayer Guide October 2, 3, 4

3 Days of Prayer and Fasting

October 2, 2024

Praying for HIM-U and the Nations

Opening Declarations

Begin with the following declarations of who we are:

  • We are a royal priesthood, a holy nation, His own special people, that we may proclaim the praises of Him who called us out of darkness into His marvelous light; 1 Peter 2:9
  • He causes us to triumph always in Christ Jesus. 2 Cor 2:14
  • The Lord reigns forever! Psalm 9:7

God's Purpose for HIM-U

"...Declaring the end from the beginning, And from ancient times things that are not yet done, Saying, 'My counsel shall stand, And I will do all My pleasure,'"

Isaiah 46:10

  • Pray that God's agenda and purpose for HIM-U will be fulfilled through us
  • Pray that we will not lose sight of the mandate – A world where no one lives and dies without Christ

The Gospel of Grace

"But none of these things move me, neither count I my life dear unto myself, so that I might finish my course with joy, and the ministry, which I have received of the Lord Jesus, to testify the gospel of the grace of God."

Acts 20:24

  • Pray that the Gospel of grace will be taught and preached throughout HIM-U churches without reservation and hesitation
  • Pray that Holy Spirit will help us to understand the Gospel of grace more fully and clearly

Preaching Christ

"Preach the word! Be ready in season and out of season. Convince, rebuke, exhort, with all longsuffering and teaching."

2 Timothy 4:2

  • Pray for the Body of Christ will arise in this nation and preach Christ alone
  • Pray for the political situation in the U.S. That God will give wisdom to those in authority and to those who advise them
  • Pray for the unity of the Body of Christ
  • Pray that the Body of Christ will be strengthened with might and power by the Holy Spirit
  • Pray that Body of Christ will understand the urgency of the time and pursue God's agenda

Prayer for America

"For kings, and for all that are in authority; that we may lead a quiet and peaceable life in all godliness and honesty."

Proverbs 21:1

  • Pray for peaceful elections
  • Pray the fear of God, discernment and wisdom for those in authority – the President, Vice-President, Congress, Judiciary, State Senate, etc
  • Pray God's protection upon the USA from all enemies

Life Abundant

"The thief does not come except to steal, and to kill, and to destroy. I have come that they may have life, and that they may have it more abundantly."

John 10:10

Declare Life abundant over all of HIM-U in this our year of LIFE ABUNDANT!

Prayer for Bishops

"And let us not grow weary while doing good, for in due season we shall reap if we do not lose heart"

Galatians 6:9

  • Pray for the Presiding Prelate (Bishop Johnson) and all the other Bishops will not grow weary in well doing
  • Declare long life, divine health, wholeness and no weapon formed against them will prosper
  • Declare they go from glory to glory and faith to faith

Resources for Ministry

"And God is able to bless you abundantly, so that in all things at all times, having all that you need, you will abound in every good work."

2 Corinthians 9:8

  • Pray for an abundance of resources for every good work the Lord has called HIM-U to do
  • Pray for that HIIAU will be trailblazer for great spiritual foundation and academic excellence
  • Pray that more schools, universities orphanages, clinics and hospitals will be built

Thanksgiving for HIM-U

"Praise the Lord..."

Psalm 111:1

  • Thank God for 34 years of existence as a ministry
  • Pray that years ahead as a ministry will be even more glorious and we will see more churches planted in the nations
  • Pray that God will raise up more evangelists and prophets in HIM-U

October 3, 2024

Praying for Harvest Olney

Invest Beyond Declarations

Begin with the following declarations:

  • Declare that God our Father is Jehovah Jireh, our Provider. We have all the finances for Invest Beyond. We see the sanctuary completed and call it so. That our God shall supply all our needs according to His riches in glory by Christ Jesus. Phil 4:19

Thanksgiving for Harvest Olney

"Praise ye the LORD. O give thanks unto the LORD; for he is good: for his mercy endureth forever."

Psalm 106:1

  • Thank God for what he has done in Harvest Olney from the beginning of the year till now
  • Thank God for salvation of souls, dedication of lives to Christ and people who have a fresh commitment to go deeper with the Lord
  • Thank God for healings, deliverances, promotions, provisions, protection, career breakthroughs, business breakthroughs etc.

Invest Beyond - Provision

"So I answered them, and said to them, 'The God of heaven Himself will prosper us; therefore, we His servants will arise and build, but you have no heritage or right or memorial in Jerusalem.'"

Nehemiah 2:20

  • Thank God for Invest Beyond and how the Lord has provided the resources since we broke ground
  • Thank God the sanctuary will be finished on schedule
  • Thank God that make those who are willing to give will be able to and those who are able will be willing to give
  • Declare we are experiencing increases in business, promotions on job and raises

Invest Beyond Team

"...Do not sorrow, for the joy of the Lord is your strength."

Nehemiah 8:10

  • Pray that Invest Beyond Team will not be weary or discouraged
  • Declare the Holy Spirit is continuously supplying strategies, strength, grace and wisdom to the Invest Beyond Team

Understanding the Purpose

"the sons of Issachar who had understanding of the times, to know what Israel ought to do, their chiefs were two hundred; and all their brethren were at their command."

1 Chronicles 12:32

  • Pray that God will give us deeper understanding of why we are building this sanctuary - for God's glory, salvation of lost souls, raising a prophetic end-time army and training and releasing men and women for the nations and harvest
  • Declare that our hearts are more united as we build this sanctuary, and God is doing a mighty work in our hearts and lives
  • Declare during the process of building this sanctuary, God is raising up a mighty army that is completely sold out to God's agenda and purposes

Builders and Laborers

"I have called by name Bezaleel the son of Uri, the son of Hur, of the tribe of Judah: And I have filled him with the spirit of God, in wisdom, and in understanding, and in knowledge, and in all manner of workmanship, To devise cunning works, to work in gold, and in silver, and in brass,"

Exodus 31:2-4

  • Pray God's wisdom and protection on the engineers, builders and laborers
  • Pray that they experience God in a new way. May unsaved get saved

Protection for the Project

"No weapon formed against you shall prosper, And every tongue which rises against you in judgment You shall condemn. This is the heritage of the servants of the Lord, And their righteousness is from Me,' Says the Lord."

Isaiah 54:17

  • Declare no weapons formed against Invest Beyond will prosper
  • Declare there will be no injury on the project site
  • Declare that the Hand of Lord is upon this project and is being built for God's glory

Resources and Favor

"I will go before thee and make the crooked places straight: I will break in pieces the gates of brass, and cut in sunder the bars of iron: And I will give thee the treasures of darkness, and hidden riches of secret places, that thou mayest know that I, the LORD, which call thee by thy name, am the God of Israel."

Isaiah 45:2-3

  • Pray we tap into resources God has designed for the completion of this building
  • Declare that we have favor with all regulatory agencies
  • Pray that this project will not be halted due to a lack of funds

Holy Spirit Baptism

"But thou shalt remember the Lord thy God: for it is he that giveth thee power to get wealth, that he may establish his covenant which he sware unto thy fathers, as it is this day."

Deuteronomy 8:18

  • Pray for more Harvesters (and those who visit regularly) to see the need to be baptized in the Holy Spirit

October 4, 2024

Praying for Harvest Olney continued...

Healing Declarations

Declarations:

  • Declare Jesus is our healer. He is still same healer 2000 years ago. Hebrew 13:8
  • Declare that the Glory of the Lord is present at Harvest Olney and fills every service; that people are being born again and baptized in the Holy Spirit every week; that no one comes and leaves in the same way because God is in our midst. Matt. 18:20

By His Stripes We Are Healed

"But He was wounded for our transgressions, He was bruised for our iniquities; the chastisement for our peace was upon Him, and by His stripes we are healed."

Isaiah 53:5

  • Declare God's healing power is manifesting in our lives
  • Declare every cell, tissue, organ and system is whole
  • Pray for healing for those who are dealing with emotional and mental issues
  • Pray that we see more testimonies of people being healed at Harvest Olney from all kinds of diseases
  • Pray the gifts of healings will operate at higher level in our services
  • Pray that we make a practice in our everyday lives to pray for the sick and see them recover

Our Identity in Christ

"giving thanks to the Father who has qualified us to be partakers of the inheritance of the saints in the light. He has delivered us from the power of darkness and conveyed us into the kingdom of the Son of His love, in whom we have redemption through His blood, the forgiveness of sins."

Colossians 1:12-14

  • Pray that God gives us a greater revelation of who we are in Christ Jesus
  • Pray that we understand more fully that we are partakers of the inheritance of saints in the light
  • Declare we have been delivered from the kingdom of darkness
  • Declare that we are in Kingdom of the Son of His love
  • Declare we have redemption through His blood and the forgiveness of sins

Favor and Provision

"For you bless the righteous, O Lord; You cover him with favor as with a shield."

Psalm 5:12

  • Pray that the Lord will give favor to Harvesters looking for jobs, needing immigration papers, needing promotions, wanting to start businesses...
  • Pray that God will give those trust Him for godly spouses their heart desires according to His will
  • Pray for married couples that are trusting the Lord for the fruit of the womb will see the manifestations

Longing for God's Presence

"As the deer pants for the water brooks, So pants my soul for You, O God. My soul thirsts for God, for the living God..."

Psalm 42:1-2

  • Pray that Harvesters will long for God's presence

God's Glory

"So that the priests could not continue ministering because of the cloud; for the glory of the Lord filled the house of God."

2 Chronicles 5:14

  • Thank God that the Lord is graciously being present in our midst
  • Pray that we see a greater manifestation of God's glory and power in our midst
  • Pray each pulpit minister will have fresh grace and anointing
  • Pray that the supernatural and miraculous will become our experience every time we gather
  • Pray that there will be greater dimension of the revelation of God's Word

Desire for the Word

"As newborn babes, desire the pure milk of the word, that you may grow thereby,"

1 Peter 2:2

  • Pray that each Harvester will sincerely desire the Word
  • Pray that we see desire to grow and mature into the image of Christ
  • Declare we refuse to remain spiritual babies

Beholding God's Glory

"But we all, with unveiled face, beholding as in a mirror the glory of the Lord, are being transformed into the same image from glory to glory, just as by the Spirit of the Lord."

2 Corinthians 3:18

  • Pray that we Harvester will behold God's glory intentionally in all situation and circumstance
  • Pray that we will behold Jesus as our healer, good shepherd, our high priest, Lamb of God etc.

Church Attendance

"And let us consider how we may spur one another on toward love and good deeds, not giving up meeting together, as some are in the habit of doing, but encouraging one another...."

Hebrews 10:24-25a

  • Pray that God will increase our attendance, bringing back those who had stopped attending and increasing the attendance of those who visit for the first time
  • Pray that God will give us "Godly", not just good strategies as our leaders plan for how to do this

Closing Prayer

CLOSING: Thank God for the assurance that as we, the righteous, have prayed in agreement in the name of Jesus according to the Word of God, He has heard and answered. Amen

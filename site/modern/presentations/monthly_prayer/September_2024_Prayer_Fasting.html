Day One

Wednesday September 5th

Declarations & Thanksgiving

Powerful & Effective Prayer

"...The prayer of a righteous person is powerful and effective."

James 5:16b (NLT)

Declare our confidence in what we are doing tonight because God's word says that our prayers are powerful and can produce wonderful results.

God Hears and Answers

"In those days when you pray, I will listen."

Jeremiah 29:12 (NLT)

"I will answer them before they even call to me. While they are still talking about their needs, I will go ahead and answer their prayers!"

Isaiah 65:24 (NLT)

Abiding in Christ

"But if you remain in me and my words remain in you, you may ask for anything you want, and it will be granted!"

John 15:7 (NLT)

DECLARE that God not only hears our prayers, but that He answers.

Exceeding Abundantly Above

"Now to Him who is able to do exceedingly abundantly above all that we ask or think, according to the power that works in us..."

Ephesians 3:20 (NKJV)

DECLARE that whatever we ask for, our God is able to do exceedingly abundantly above that.

That all things are possible for those who believe in His mighty power which is at work in us.

DECLARE that He is not just willing, but that He is able.

Blessed Youth and Adults

"Blessed is the man who walks not in the counsel of the ungodly, nor stands in the path of sinners, nor sits in the seat of the scornful; but his delight is in the law of the LORD, and in His law he meditates day and night. He shall be like a tree planted by the rivers of water, that brings forth its fruit in its season, whose leaf also shall not wither; and whatever he does shall prosper."

Psalm 1:1-3 (NKJV)

Declaration for Harvest Olney

DECLARE that our youth and adults at Harvest Olney are blessed:

  • That they do not walk in the counsel of the ungodly
  • That they will delight in the Word of God and hide it in their hearts
  • That they will be fruitful in the things of the Lord
  • That they will prosper in what they put their hands to do

God's Ultimate Provision

"He who did not spare His own Son, but delivered Him up for us all, how shall He not with Him also freely give us all things?"

Romans 8:32 (NKJV)

THANK God for His ultimate provision

DECLARE that if He could deliver His own son for us, what can He not do for us....

Our Refuge and Blessing

"Keep me safe, O God, for I have come to you for refuge."

Psalm 16:1

DECLARE that God keeps us safe and that He is our refuge, our hiding place.

"I said to the Lord, 'You are my Master! Every good thing I have comes from you.'"

Psalm 16:2

DECLARE that we have good things and that they all come from the Lord our God.

Our Inheritance

"Lord, you alone are my inheritance, my cup of blessing. You guard all that is mine."

Psalm 16:5

DECLARE that we have an inheritance from the Lord that he guards all that He has given to us.

"I know the Lord is always with me. I will not be shaken, for he is right beside me."

Psalm 16:8

DECLARE that God is our ever present help; declare his name, Jehovah EZER, the Lord our helper.

God's Word Never Barren

"It is the same with my word. I send it out, and it always produces fruit. It will accomplish all I want it to, and it will prosper everywhere I send it."

Isaiah 55:10-11

THANK God for His Word:

  • That it always produces fruit
  • That it is never barren
  • That it accomplishes all He wants it to accomplish
  • That it prospers everywhere he sends it

Year of Life Abundant

"The thief comes only to steal and kill and destroy; I have come that they may have life, and have it to the full."

John 10:10

DECLARE that in this our Year of Life Abundant, we shall focus not on the thief, but on the one who comes at the same time with His life abundant.

Day Two

Thursday September 6th

Harvest Olney & USA

Power of the Spirit

"And my speech and my preaching was not with enticing words of man's wisdom, but in demonstration of the Spirit and of power...."

1 Corinthians 2:4

THANK God for Bishop Johnson and Pastor Chrys, our senior pastors, and for all our pastors who bring the Word to us and pray that all who stand behind Harvest Olney's pulpit will not speak with "enticing words of man's wisdom, but in the demonstration of the Spirit and of power...."

Wisdom from God

"For wisdom is better than jewels, and all that you may desire cannot compare with her."

Proverbs 8:11

"If any of you lacks wisdom, you should ask God, who gives generously to all without finding fault, and it will be given to you."

James 1:5

THANK God for this wisdom that comes from Him.

Pray for wisdom for senior pastors and all our leaders at Harvest Olney.

Spiritual Wisdom and Insight

"...asking God, the glorious Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, to give you spiritual wisdom and insight so that you might grow in your knowledge of God. I pray that your hearts will be flooded with light so that you can understand the confident hope he has given to those he calledâ€”his holy people who are his rich and glorious inheritance."

Ephesians 1:17-18

Prayer for Harvest Olney

PRAY:

  • That God will give us spiritual wisdom so that Harvesters will grow in our knowledge of God; that we come to know not just His acts, but his ways
  • That we will understand the hope we have in Him

Complete Knowledge of His Will

"So we have not stopped praying for you since we first heard about you. We ask God to give you complete knowledge of his will and to give you spiritual wisdom and understanding. Then the way you live will always honor and please the Lord, and your lives will produce every kind of good fruit. All the while, you will grow as you learn to know God better and better."

Colossians 1:9-10

Prayers for Fruitful Living

PRAY that we have complete knowledge of His will for us

PRAY that we have spiritual wisdom and understanding so that:

  • We can live in a way that always honors and pleases our Lord
  • Our lives will produce every kind of good fruit
  • We will grow as we learn to know God better and better

Doers of the Word

"For if you listen to the word and don't obey, it is like glancing at your face in a mirror. You see yourself, walk away, and forget what you look like."

James 1:23-24

DECLARE that this is not our portion; we will read and listen to the Word and we will do what it says. Amen!

Grace and Peace

"Let your conversation be always full of grace, seasoned with salt, so that you may know how to answer everyone."

Colossians 4:6

PRAY for wisdom to deal with, to live in peace with each other.

Inner Strength

"I pray that from his glorious, unlimited resources He will empower you with inner strength through his Spirit."

Ephesians 3:16 (NLT)

PRAY this for us at Harvest Olney, that we be empowered with inner strength.

Love Each Other

"Dear friends, since God loved us that much, we surely ought to love each other."

1 John 4:11

PRAY that we will build our capacity to love each other, in word, but more importantly in deed, in demonstration by what we do.

New Strength

"Have you never heard? Have you never understood? The Lord is the everlasting God, the Creator of all the earth. He never grows weak or weary. No one can measure the depths of his understanding. He gives power to the weak and strength to the powerless. Even youths will become weak and tired, and young men will fall in exhaustion. But those who trust in the Lord will find new strength. They will soar high on wings like eagles. They will run and not grow weary. They will walk and not faint."

Isaiah 40:28-31

Declaration for Those in Trials

DECLARE over those among us who are in trials and tribulations and pain and hurt:

  • That their God never grows weary
  • That He gives power to the weak and strength to the powerless
  • That as they continue to trust in Him, new strength to continue to stand

Prayer for America

"And work for the peace and prosperity of the city where I sent you into exile. Pray to the Lord for it, for its welfare will determine your welfare."

Jeremiah 29:7

Pray in the Spirit for America!!

Closing Prayer

THANK GOD FOR HEARING AND ANSWERING US!!

A Gift For You

Bishop Darlingston Johnson

    A GIFT FOR YOU

    Bishop Darlingston Johnson

    December 29, 2025

    "For there is born to you this day in the city of David, a Savior who is Christ the Lord."

    - Luke 2:10-11

    Jesus Christ is God's Personal Gift to You

    "Then the angel said to them, 'Do not be afraid, for behold, I bring you good tidings of great joy which will be to all people. For there is born to you this day in the city of David, a Savior who is Christ the Lord.'"
    Luke 2:10-11
    December 29, 2025

    BIG IDEA

    Jesus Christ is God's personal gift to each individual - a gift that must be unwrapped, received personally, and celebrated as the fulfillment of God's eternal promise.

    December 29, 2025

    GOD CHOOSES THE EXCLUDED

    God deliberately chose shepherds - society's rejects, the unclean, those looked down upon - to receive history's most important announcement. This reveals God's heart: He doesn't value people according to worldly titles, education, possessions, or positions. Every person has equal worth and value in God's eyes.

    December 29, 2025

    UNTO YOU

    Your Name is on the Gift

    The angel said "unto YOU" - not just to all people, but to YOU personally. Many believers hear God's promises but disqualify themselves based on self-evaluation or comparison with others. Jesus is the gift to all people, but He is also God's gift to ME. Your name is on that gift.

    December 29, 2025

    Unwrap the Gift

    Many Christians lack joy not because they don't have the gift, but because they haven't unwrapped it.

    • Take possession of Jesus personally
    • Appropriate what He has given you
    • Experience the fullness of who He is
    • The greater the revelation, the greater the joy
    December 29, 2025

    ROOTED IN HISTORY

    Not Myth, But Reality

    This is not abstract philosophy or myth. On a certain day in history, during King Augustus's reign, in Bethlehem, a baby was born. Shepherds saw Him. He lived, performed miracles, was crucified, died, and rose on the third day. There were eyewitnesses. Jesus is a real Savior who provides real salvation for real people with real needs.

    December 29, 2025

    "HE WILL COME" TO "HE HAS COME"

    For 5,000 years God promised "He will come." Then came 400 years of silence. But at the fullness of time, "He will come" changed to "He has come." Over 400 prophecies fulfilled to the letter. Silence doesn't mean God has forgotten. No word of God is void of power. It is impossible for God's word not to come to pass.

    December 29, 2025

    A "This Day" God

    While we can look back and forward, our God is a "THIS DAY" God:

    • This day, Christ is your Savior
    • This day, Christ is your Lord
    • This day, God is answering prayer
    • This day, God is working miracles
    • This day, God is being faithful

    We serve a right now God and an on-time God.

    December 29, 2025

    THE GIFT IS GOD

    In Christ, the giver is God and the gift is God. God has not only given you a gift - God has given you God. If God has given you God, then this day God has given you the answer (because God IS the answer), the breakthrough (because God IS the breakthrough), the healer and the healing (because God IS).

    December 29, 2025

    Prayer Points

    Let us pray together, receiving and unwrapping God's gift of Jesus Christ.

    Prayer for Recognition of Personal Value

    Father, we thank You that You don't value us according to titles, education, possessions, or social standing. Just as You chose shepherds to receive the announcement of Jesus' birth, You choose us. Forgive us for times we've felt inferior or superior. Help us to stand straight, knowing we are just as valuable in Your eyes as anyone else. Break off every chain of comparison and every root of pride. Help us to stop excluding ourselves when You have included us.

    Luke 2:10-11 - God sent the most important announcement to shepherds, society's rejects; 1 Samuel 16:7 - "The Lord looks at the heart"

    Prayer for Personal Appropriation of Jesus

    Lord Jesus, we declare today that You are not just the gift to the world - You are the gift to ME. My name is on this gift of salvation. I refuse to exclude myself from Your promises. Though You love the whole world, You also love me individually and personally. I receive You, I take possession of You, I appropriate You, and I will begin to enjoy the fullness of all that You are. Jesus, You are MY Savior, MY Lord, MY answer, MY everything.

    Luke 2:10-11 - "Unto YOU is born this day a Savior"; John 1:12 - "As many as received Him, to them He gave the right to become children of God"

    Prayer for Help in Unwrapping the Gift

    Holy Spirit, help me to unwrap the gift of Jesus so I can truly see what I have. I confess that I've left so much of Jesus wrapped up. Like a child surprised at how big the gift is once unwrapped, surprise me with the magnitude of who Jesus is. Give me revelation upon revelation of His love, power, presence, and provision. Thank You for sending helpers to lead me into all truth. Open my eyes to see Jesus more clearly. The greater my revelation of who He is, the greater my joy will be.

    Ephesians 1:17-18 - "That God may give you the spirit of wisdom and revelation in the knowledge of Him, the eyes of your understanding being enlightened"

    Prayer for Trusting God's Faithfulness

    Lord, I thank You that Your word is sure. For 5,000 years You promised "He will come." After 400 years of silence, "He will come" became "He has come." Father, I'm in a season where I've heard from You but now there's silence. This story encourages me. No word of Yours is void of power. It is IMPOSSIBLE for Your word not to come to pass. I will not doubt during the silence. I will meditate on Your promises. What You promised me WILL come to pass.

    Isaiah 55:11 - "My word shall not return void"; Numbers 23:19 - "God is not a man, that He should lie. Has He said, and will He not do?"

    Prayer for Bold Witness and Proclamation

    Lord, like the shepherds who believed the good news, went to see, and then told everyone, help me to respond rightly to this gift of Jesus. I believe this good news - that unto me is born this day a Savior who is Christ the Lord. I will act upon this truth. And I will not keep my mouth shut. I will tell the whole world because Jesus is not just a gift for me - He's a gift for all people. Give me boldness to proclaim the good news. Let my life and words point others to the Savior.

    Luke 2:17-18 - "When they had seen Him, they made widely known the saying concerning this Child"; Mark 16:15 - "Go into all the world and preach the gospel"
    December 29, 2025

    Our Response

    Like the shepherds, we must:

    • Believe the good news that Jesus is our personal Savior
    • Act upon this truth
    • Tell the whole world - proclaim it
    • Receive Jesus as both Savior and Lord

    Only as Lord can He save us.

    THANK YOU

    Bishop Darlingston Johnson

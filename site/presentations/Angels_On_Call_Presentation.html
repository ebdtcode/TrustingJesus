    ANGELS ON CALL

    Bishop Dr. Darlingston Johnson

    Understanding the Ministry of Angels

    December 21, 2025

    Ministering Spirits Sent to Serve You

    "Are they not all ministering spirits sent forth to minister for those who will inherit salvation?"
    Hebrews 1:14
    December 21, 2025

    WE ARE NOT ALONE

    Many believers live as though heaven is quiet and help is scarce. We pray as if God is far away and face battles as though we are alone and unsupported.

    But Scripture reveals something radically different: angels are among us, on assignment, actively ministering to the heirs of salvation.

    December 21, 2025

    What Are Angels?

    • Spiritual beings created by God
    • Created to serve God
    • Extremely powerful
    • Commanded by God to serve you

    Angels are a covenant blessing for ALL of God's children.

    December 21, 2025

    Three Roles of Angels

    • Heaven's Worshipers - They behold God and worship Him continually
    • Heaven's Warriors - Fighting battles in the unseen realm so we are not overwhelmed
    • Protective Services - Rendering service on your behalf as an heir of salvation
    December 21, 2025

    POWERFUL WARRIORS

    One Angel = 185,000 Soldiers

    When King Hezekiah prayed, God sent just ONE angel who killed 185,000 enemy soldiers in a single night.

    "Imagine such powerful creatures created by God to serve Him. He has charged them to minister to you as an heir of salvation."

    December 21, 2025

    UNDER AUTHORITY

    Angels operate legally. They don't act independently or respond to emotions.

    They are servants under authority, responding only to the voice of God's Word.

    Those who experience greater angelic activity are more aligned with the spiritual laws that angels obey.

    "Bless the Lord, you His angels, who excel in strength, who do His word, heeding the voice of His word."
    Psalm 103:20
    December 21, 2025

    Activating Angelic Ministry

    • Speak God's Word - Voice the word consistently, not just think it
    • Pray Based on the Word - Prayer and the Word create legal authority
    • Declare God's Promises - Say "The Lord is my refuge," "No weapon formed against me will prosper"
    • Don't Be Silent - Angels wait for the spoken Word to act
    December 21, 2025

    YOUR WORDS CREATE LEGAL ENVIRONMENTS

    When you speak God's Word: Angels have legal authority to enforce it.

    When you speak fear and doubt: You create a legal environment for demons.

    "The word spoken creates the legal environment and gives angels the legal authority they need to act."

    Prayer for Awareness of Angelic Ministry

    Father, I thank You for the ministry of angels. I acknowledge that I am never alone and never without resources. Angels are present with me right now, charged by You to minister on my behalf. I will not live as though heaven is quiet or help is scarce. I declare that I am an heir of salvation, and angels are my covenant blessing.

    "Are they not all ministering spirits sent forth to minister for those who will inherit salvation?" - Hebrews 1:14

    Prayer for Consistency in Speaking God's Word

    Lord, I will not be silent. I will give voice to Your Word consistently. I will create the legal environment that allows angels to function freely in my life. I declare: The Lord is my refuge and my fortress. No weapon formed against me will prosper. My God supplies all my needs according to His riches in glory. Angels, you have the legal authority to enforce these words in Jesus' name.

    "Bless the Lord, you His angels, who excel in strength, who do His word, heeding the voice of His word." - Psalm 103:20

    Prayer for Angelic Protection

    Heavenly Father, I thank You that angels are fighting battles in the unseen so that I will not be overwhelmed in the seen. Thank You for protective services that surpass even presidential security. I ask for the ministry of angels to strengthen me when I am weary, to guide me when I need direction, and to protect me from seen and unseen dangers. I receive their ministry now in Jesus' name.

    "He shall give His angels charge over you, to keep you in all your ways." - Psalm 91:11
    December 21, 2025

    Remember

    You have angels present with you every day. They are busy on your behalf, though you may not always see them.

    "As great as angels are, there's One far greater. Thank God for angels, but your greatest gift is Jesus."

    December 21, 2025

Angels On Call

Ministering Spirits Sent to Serve You

"Are they not all ministering spirits sent forth to minister for those who will inherit salvation?"
Hebrews 1:14 (NKJV)
Bishop Dr. Darlingston Johnson
December 21, 2025

Key Scripture

"Are they not all ministering spirits sent forth to minister for those who will inherit salvation?"
Hebrews 1:14 (NKJV)
Angels are spiritual beings created by God to serve God, and God has commanded them to serve you.

We Are Not Alone

  • Many believers live as though heaven is quiet and help is scarce
  • We pray as if God is far away
  • We face battles as though we are alone and unsupported
  • But scripture reveals something radically different
  • There are angels among us on assignment right now
  • In this room, there are probably more angels than human beings
"The scriptures reveal that we are not alone"

What Are Angels?

  • Angels are spiritual beings created by God to serve God
  • They are extremely powerful
  • We cannot see them ordinarily, but they can manifest physically
  • They are not people who died and became angels
  • You had angels assigned to you long before you were born
  • God created angels and assigned them to care for His children

Angels are a covenant blessing - they are your inheritance

Three Roles of Angels

How Angels Serve God and You

Heaven's Worshipers

  • Worship is probably the most important thing angels do
  • They behold God and worship Him continually
  • Angels are very aware they are not to be worshiped
  • All worship belongs to the Lord alone
  • Angels always point to God, never to themselves
  • We worship God the Father, God the Son, and God the Holy Spirit
"Even John the Apostle fell down before an angel, and the angel said: 'Don't do that! Worship God!'"

Heaven's Warriors

  • Angels fight battles in the unseen realm
  • Thank God we don't know all the battles being fought
  • They fight so we cannot be overwhelmed in the seen
  • Some battles are about your future and destiny
  • Angels are extremely powerful warriors
"That night one angel killed 185,000 soldiers in the camp of the Assyrian army."
2 Kings 19:35
Just one angel. Imagine such powerful creatures assigned to serve you!

Protective Services

  • Angels provide protection every day
  • They minister to us physically when we are weary
  • Angels strengthened Jesus after His 40-day fast
  • Angels strengthened Jesus in the Garden of Gethsemane
  • An angel fed Elijah and strengthened him for 40 days
  • God has charged them to bear us up in their hands

You think the President is the only one with secret service? You don't know whose children you are!

How Angels Operate

Understanding Angelic Activity

Angels Operate Legally

  • Angels don't act independently or based on emotions
  • They are servants under authority
  • There are spiritual laws that govern their activity
  • They don't respond to tears, complaining, or need alone
  • Angels wait for legal authority to act
  • Like a well-trained army waiting for orders
"Angels may see you need help, but they can't move until they have the word. They need legal authority to act."

Angels Heed the Voice of His Word

"Bless the Lord, you His angels, who excel in strength, who do His word, heeding the voice of His word."
Psalm 103:20 (NKJV)
  • Angels heed the voice of God's word
  • Not just the word on the page
  • Not the word in your mind
  • The word must be spoken
  • When we speak God's word, angels get legal authority to act

Activating Angelic Ministry

Create Legal Environment

  • Voice and declare God's word
  • Open your mouth and speak it
  • Speak consistently every day
  • Take promises and declare them
  • Align with spiritual laws angels obey

Pray Based on God's Word

  • Angels respond to believing prayer
  • No prayer means no angelic ministry
  • Prayer plus God's word creates conditions
  • Angels were dispatched the moment Daniel prayed
  • Keep believing and standing in faith

The Power of Your Words

"Death and life are in the power of the tongue."
Proverbs 18:21 (NKJV)
  • When you say "The Lord is my refuge," angels enforce that
  • When you say "No weapon formed against me will prosper," they act
  • When you say "My God will supply all my needs," they move
  • You create legal environment that allows them to function
  • Your words give life to God's will in your life

Don't confuse your angels - speak God's word consistently!

Prayer for Awareness

Father God, I thank You for the ministry of angels in my life. Open my eyes to see that I am not alone, not unsupported, not without help. Thank You that right now, angels are present with me on assignment. Help me to be more aware of their presence and activity. I acknowledge that angels are a covenant blessing and my inheritance as a child of God. I will not live as though heaven is quiet or help is scarce. I declare that angels are ministering spirits sent to serve me as an heir of salvation. Thank You for blessing me with a host of angels whose job is to look after me, care for me, and be present with me.
Hebrews 1:14; Psalm 103:20

Prayer for Speaking God's Word

Lord Jesus, I understand that angels heed the voice of Your word. Help me to be consistent in voicing and declaring Your word over my life. I will not be silent. I will open my mouth and speak Your promises. I will create the legal environment that allows angels to function freely in my life. I resist the temptation to give life to fears, anxieties, and negative thoughts with my words. Instead, I choose to declare: The Lord is my refuge and my fortress. No weapon formed against me will prosper. My God will supply all my needs according to His riches in glory. I am more than a conqueror through Christ.
Psalm 103:20; Proverbs 18:21; Romans 8:37

Prayer for Increased Angelic Activity

Heavenly Father, I recognize that prayer activates angelic ministry in my life. Like Jesus who said He could ask and 72,000 angels would come, I come to You now asking for the full measure of angelic assistance You have provided for me. I don't believe the lie that I am alone or under-resourced. I pray believing prayers based on Your word. Like Daniel who prayed and angels were immediately dispatched, I stand in faith knowing that as soon as I pray according to Your will, angels move on my behalf. I will back up my prayers by declaring Your word consistently. Increase angelic activity in my life for Your glory.
Daniel 10; Matthew 26:53; James 5:16

Remember This

  • Angels are present with you right now
  • They are waiting to serve you as an heir of salvation
  • They operate legally under God's authority
  • Your words and prayers activate their ministry
  • Speak God's word consistently to create legal environment
  • Angels are your covenant blessing and inheritance

As Great As Angels Are, There Is One Far Greater: Jesus

Learn from the angels to bow before Him

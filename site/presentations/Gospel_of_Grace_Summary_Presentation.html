Six Pillars of Peace

Our Unshakeable Foundation

Building a life of confidence in God's unchanging love

Pillar 1: Peace Through Christ Alone

  • Jesus is God's Peace Child - the divine solution to war between God and humanity
  • The war that lasted for thousands of years has ended
  • As long as Jesus lives, God is at peace with believers
  • Accepting the Peace Child ends the war forever
  • He Himself IS our peace with God

Pillar 2: The Legal Basis of Peace

  • Our peace is not emotional but based on solid legal foundation
  • Christ fulfilled every requirement of God's law perfectly
  • The heavenly court has declared us "NOT GUILTY"
  • We have a legal deed to this peace - God's Word
  • No one can challenge what God has legally established

Pillar 3: Peace Is More Than the End of War

"The bombs have stopped falling"
  • God is completely pleased with you in Christ
  • He has nothing against you in His heart
  • All charges have been dropped completely
  • God is not withholding His love or favor
  • Complete restoration and acceptance

Pillar 4: Our Justification Is Secure

"Who shall bring a charge against God's elect? It is God who justifies. Who is he who condemns? It is Christ who died... who also makes intercession for us."
Romans 8:33-34
  • No accusation can stand against us
  • Christ died and rose again as our defense
  • Nothing can separate us from God's love

Pillar 5: Living in Grace

"Through whom also we have access by faith into this grace in which we stand"
  • Grace doesn't leave you the way it found you
  • You become a new creation in Christ
  • Grace teaches you to say no to sin
  • God works in you both to will and to do
  • Transformation happens from the inside out

Pillar 6: The Father's Heart

"God treats us as though we never left home"
  • The Father runs to meet us while we are yet far off
  • Complete restoration with the best robe, ring, and shoes
  • Treated exactly as He would treat Jesus
  • As though you never sinned or rebelled
  • With complete acceptance and joy

Practical Applications

Living Out Your Peace

How this truth transforms every area of life

Daily Living Applications

Personal Identity

  • See yourself as God sees you
  • Rest in His complete acceptance
  • Live from approval, not for approval
  • Stand confidently in His love

Facing Challenges

  • "If God is for us, who can be against us?"
  • No trial can separate from His love
  • Draw strength from secure foundation
  • Face death with confidence

Prayer Point 1: Thanksgiving for Peace

"Father, thank You that Jesus is our Peace Child. The war between us has ended forever. We praise You that this peace rests on the solid legal foundation of Christ's perfect work, not on our emotions or performance."
Based on Romans 5:1

Prayer Point 2: Confidence in God's Love

"Lord, when circumstances overwhelm us, help us boldly declare: 'If God is for us, who can be against us?' Strengthen our confidence that nothing can separate us from Your love in Christ Jesus."
Based on Romans 8:31, 38-39

Prayer Point 3: Freedom from Condemnation

"We declare that there is no condemnation for those in Christ Jesus. When guilt tries to overwhelm us, remind us that You justify us, not condemn us. No accusation can stand against Your elect."
Based on Romans 8:1, 33-34

Prayer Point 4: Grace for Transformation

"Lord, let Your grace work powerfully in us, changing our desires and transforming us from glory to glory. Thank You that grace doesn't leave us as it found us but makes us new creations in Christ."
Based on Romans 5:2, 2 Corinthians 5:17

Prayer Point 5: Boldness in Witnessing

"Father, this gospel is too good to keep to ourselves. Give us joy and excitement about Your grace that motivates us to share. May we be motivated from within to tell others about Jesus, our Peace Child."
Based on 2 Corinthians 5:18-20

Declaration of Faith

"Jesus is the Peace Child. The war has ended. The bombs have stopped falling. God treats me as though I never left home. This peace is mine legally. I am at peace with God forever."
This is not wishful thinking - this is legal reality in Christ Jesus.

  • "God treats us as though we never left home" - Complete restoration

Living the Higher Life

"If then you were raised with Christ, seek those things which are above, where Christ is, sitting at the right hand of God. Set your mind on things above, not on things on the earth."
Colossians 3:1-2
Bishop Darlingston Johnson
A Message on Embracing Your Identity in Christ

Three Marks of Spiritual Growth

What God Celebrates in Us
  • Faith in Christ - Not faith in ourselves or circumstances, but confidence in who Jesus is
  • Love for One Another - Supernatural love that goes beyond preference and convenience
  • Everlasting Hope - An anchor that keeps us steadfast no matter what life brings
"When these three qualities are active and growing, we are experiencing true spiritual maturity"

Living from Above

The Airplane Illustration

Above the Storm

"At 10,000 feet, it's smooth sailing above the turbulent weather below"
  • The weather below didn't change - the altitude did
  • Our circumstances may not change, but our response can
  • When we live from our position in Christ, we respond from peace
  • Higher is better - smoother above the turbulence

Stop Eating Worms

"You're not a chicken - you're an eagle!"
  • An eagle raised among chickens ate worms from the dust
  • When it discovered its wings, everything changed
  • You were not designed for earthly living but heavenly living
  • Called to live above the flesh, above sin, above this world system

Three Practical Steps

How to Live the Higher Life

Living It Out Daily

1. Seek Things Above

  • That's where we spiritually live
  • That's where the best things are
  • Our spiritual address is "in Christ"

2. Set Your Mind Above

  • Meditate on God's Word
  • "Change the channel" of thoughts
  • Use your mouth as a remote control

3. Mortify the Deeds of the Flesh

Discard the old furniture left by the previous occupant. The old man is dead - now remove what he left behind and bring in new things from Christ.

Using Your Spiritual Remote

"Click - change the channel!"
  • When negative thoughts come, don't receive them
  • Speak the truth: "I am in Christ"
  • "My life is complete in Him"
  • "Everything He is, I am. Everything He has, I have"
  • "I'm living from above"

The Names of God

And Their Meaning

150+ Biblical Names with Scripture References

Throughout Scripture, God reveals Himself through His names. Each name unveils an aspect of His character, His relationship with His people, and His redemptive work. Understanding these names deepens our worship, strengthens our faith, and transforms how we approach God in prayer. This comprehensive study covers Hebrew names, compound names, New Testament names of Jesus, and divine titles found throughout the Bible.

Primary Names of God

Elohim (אלהים)
"God" - The Supreme Creator
The plural form emphasizes God's majesty, power, and the Trinity. Used over 2,500 times in Scripture, this name reveals God as the all-powerful Creator who spoke the universe into existence.
"In the beginning God [Elohim] created the heavens and the earth."
Genesis 1:1 (NIV)
YHWH / Yahweh (יהוה)
"I AM" - The Self-Existent One
The most sacred name of God, revealed to Moses at the burning bush. This covenant name emphasizes God's eternal, self-existent nature - He was, is, and always will be. Rendered as "LORD" in English.
"God said to Moses, 'I AM WHO I AM. This is what you are to say to the Israelites: I AM has sent me to you.'"
Exodus 3:14 (NIV)
Adonai (אדני)
"Lord" - Master, Owner
Acknowledges God's authority and lordship over all creation. Occurs 434 times in the Old Testament. When we call God "Adonai," we recognize His right to rule our lives completely.
"O Sovereign LORD, you have begun to show to your servant your greatness and your strong hand."
Deuteronomy 3:24 (NIV)
El (אל)
"God" - The Mighty One
The singular form of Elohim, emphasizing God's strength and power. Often used in compound names to describe specific attributes of God. El is the strong God who fulfills His promises.
"The Mighty One, God, the LORD, speaks and summons the earth."
Psalm 50:1 (NIV)

El Compound Names (18 Names)

El Shaddai (אל שדי)
"God Almighty" - All-Sufficient One
Reveals God as the all-sufficient, almighty God who supplies all our needs. He nourishes, satisfies, and supplies His people with invincible strength.
Genesis 17:1; Psalm 91:1
El Elyon (אל עליון)
"God Most High" - Supreme Ruler
Declares that God is exalted above all gods, powers, and authorities. None is higher than El Elyon - He rules with absolute sovereignty.
Genesis 14:18-20; Psalm 7:17
El Roi (אל ראי)
"The God Who Sees"
Named by Hagar in the wilderness. God sees our circumstances, pain, struggles, and needs. We are never invisible or forgotten.
Genesis 16:13-14
El Olam (אל עולם)
"The Everlasting God"
Reveals God's eternal nature - no beginning, no end. His love, faithfulness, and promises endure forever beyond all time.
Genesis 21:33; Isaiah 40:28
El Gibhor (אל גבור)
"Mighty God"
The Mighty God who rescues and delivers. A messianic title pointing to Christ's power to save and conquer.
Isaiah 9:6
El Qanna (אל קנא)
"Jealous God"
God's zealous protection of His relationship with His people. His jealousy is holy - He will not share His glory with idols.
Exodus 20:5; 34:14
El Chaiyai (אל חיי)

The source and sustainer of all life. Every breath we take is a gift from El Chaiyai.

Set apart, pure, and morally perfect. There is no comparison to the Holy One of Israel.

Omniscient God who knows all things - past, present, future. Nothing is hidden from Him.

Yahweh/Jehovah Compound Names (16 Names)

Yahweh Yireh (יהוה יראה)
"The LORD Will Provide"
Abraham named the place where God provided a ram in place of Isaac. God sees our needs before we do and provides at the right time.
"So Abraham called that place The LORD Will Provide. And to this day it is said, 'On the mountain of the LORD it will be provided.'"
Genesis 22:14 (NIV)
Yahweh Rapha (יהוה רפא)
"The LORD Who Heals"
God revealed Himself as Healer after the Exodus. Yahweh Rapha heals physically, emotionally, spiritually, and relationally.
"...for I am the LORD, who heals you."
Exodus 15:26 (NIV)
Yahweh Nissi (יהוה נסי)
"The LORD Is My Banner"
After victory over the Amalekites, Moses built this altar. God is our rallying point and source of triumph.
Exodus 17:15
Yahweh Shalom (יהוה שלום)
"The LORD Is Peace"
Gideon built this altar when God gave him peace. Shalom means completeness, wholeness, and prosperity.
Judges 6:24
Yahweh Rohi (יהוה רעי)
"The LORD Is My Shepherd"
David understood God as the perfect Shepherd who guides, protects, and provides for His flock.
Psalm 23:1
Yahweh Tsidkenu (יהוה צדקנו)
"The LORD Our Righteousness"
Prophetic name pointing to Christ, through whom we receive righteousness by faith.
Jeremiah 23:6
Yahweh Shammah (יהוה שמה)

Name of the restored Jerusalem. God's abiding presence is with His people always.
Ezekiel 48:35
Yahweh Sabaoth (יהוה צבאות)

Commander of heavenly armies. He leads angelic hosts and fights for His people.

Yahweh M'Kaddesh (יהוה מקדש)

God sets His people apart, making them holy for His purposes and glory.

Yahweh Bore

The Creator of the ends of the earth, who speaks worlds into existence.

Yahweh Baal Perez
"LORD of Breakthrough"

Yahweh Kesel
"The LORD My Confidence"
Our trust and confidence rest securely in the LORD alone.
Proverbs 3:26
Yahweh Mephalti
"The LORD My Deliverer"
God rescues us from trouble, enemies, and the power of sin.
Psalm 18:2; 32:7
Yahweh Shomreka
"The LORD Your Keeper"
God watches over and protects His people day and night.
Psalm 121:5

"The LORD Our Warrior"
God fights battles on behalf of His people with mighty power.

The Seven "I AM" Statements of Jesus

I AM the Bread of Life
Spiritual Sustenance
Jesus sustains spiritual life just as bread sustains physical life. Whoever comes to Him will never go hungry.
"I am the bread of life. Whoever comes to me will never go hungry, and whoever believes in me will never be thirsty."
John 6:35 (NIV)
I AM the Light of the World
Divine Illumination
Jesus dispels darkness of sin, ignorance, and death. Those who follow Him have the light of life.
"I am the light of the world. Whoever follows me will never walk in darkness, but will have the light of life."
John 8:12 (NIV)
I AM the Gate
The Door to Salvation
Jesus is the only entrance to salvation and abundant life.
John 10:9
I AM the Good Shepherd
Sacrificial Protector
Lays down His life for the sheep, unlike hired hands who abandon.
John 10:11
I AM the Resurrection and the Life
Conqueror of Death
Those who believe in Him will live even though they die.
John 11:25
I AM the Way, Truth, and Life
The Only Path to God
No one comes to the Father except through Jesus - the exclusive way to God.
John 14:6
I AM the True Vine
Source of Spiritual Life
We must remain connected to Jesus to bear fruit. Apart from Him we can do nothing.
John 15:1, 5

Prayer Focus: Knowing God Through His Names

Elohim, Creator God: Father, I worship You as the Creator of all things. Help me to trust in Your power to create new beginnings in my life. You who spoke the universe into existence can speak transformation into my circumstances.

Yahweh, I AM: Eternal God, You are the same yesterday, today, and forever. In a world of constant change, anchor me in Your unchanging nature. Be my I AM in every situation I face.

Yahweh Yireh, My Provider: Lord, I trust You to provide for all my needs according to Your riches in glory. You see what I need before I ask. Help me to rest in Your faithful provision.

Yahweh Rapha, My Healer: Healing God, I bring my brokenness to You - physical, emotional, spiritual, and relational. Touch me with Your healing power and make me whole.

Yahweh Shalom, My Peace: Lord of Peace, in the midst of life's storms, be my calm. Guard my heart and mind with Your peace that surpasses all understanding.

El Roi, The God Who Sees: You see me, Lord. You know my struggles, fears, hopes, and needs. Thank You that I am never invisible, never forgotten, never alone.

Immanuel, God With Us: Thank You, Jesus, for coming to be with us. Help me to live in constant awareness of Your presence. You are with me always, even to the end of the age.

Good Shepherd: Lead me, Lord, in paths of righteousness for Your name's sake. Protect me from the enemy, restore my soul, and cause me to dwell in Your house forever.

Alpha and Omega: You are the Beginning and the End. Help me trust that You hold my life from start to finish. Nothing surprises You; my times are in Your hands.

King of Kings: I bow before Your majesty and declare that You alone are Lord. Rule in my heart, my home, and my life. May Your kingdom come and Your will be done.

Know His Name, Know His Nature

As you study and meditate on these 150+ names of God, let them transform how you pray, how you worship, and how you live. Each name is an invitation to know God more deeply and trust Him more completely. He has revealed Himself through His names - will you draw near?

Our Foundation Scripture

"What shall we say then? Shall we continue in sin that grace may abound? Certainly not! How shall we who died to sin live any longer in it? Or do you not know that as many of us as were baptized into Christ Jesus were baptized into His death? Therefore we were buried with Him through baptism into death, that just as Christ was raised from the dead by the glory of the Father, even so we also should walk in newness of life."
Romans 6:1-4

The Frustrating Cycle

  • You sin
  • You feel guilty
  • You repent
  • You try harder not to sin again
  • You sin again
  • The cycle repeats endlessly

It feels like you can never win the battle

The Secret to Victory

"The secret to victory isn't about you mustering more willpower. The Christian life is less about striving, and more about letting and not letting."
Key Principle

The battle has already been won by Christ
Victory is not something you fight for
It's something you LET happen

Letting vs. Forcing

Forcing

  • Human effort
  • Willpower
  • Striving
  • Fighting for victory
  • Exhausting

Letting

  • Faith in Christ
  • Believing God's Word
  • Resting in victory
  • Standing in victory
  • Supernatural

Why We Still Struggle

Two Reasons:

  • IGNORANCE - You don't know what Christ has already done
  • UNBELIEF - You've heard it but choose not to believe it
"This is the victory that overcomes the world, our faith"
1 John 5:4

Identification & Union with Christ

This is not just a metaphor
This is not positive thinking
This is spiritual reality

You are ACTUALLY, VITALLY joined to Christ
Spiritual Reality

What "In Christ" Means

  • When Christ died, you died
  • When Christ was buried, you were buried
  • When Christ rose, you rose
  • When Christ defeated sin, Satan, and death, you defeated them
  • When Christ ascended, you were seated with Him

His story becomes YOUR story

The Book and Handkerchief

A handkerchief placed inside a book

  • Where the book goes, the handkerchief goes
  • The book's history becomes the handkerchief's history
  • They are inseparable

YOU are in CHRIST
Everywhere He went, you went

The Real Problem

"The problem of the sinner is not that he's bad. The problem of the sinner is that he's dead. And the only thing that can help a dead man is life."
Key Truth

God's entire plan was to give us LIFE

The Solution: LIFE

"I have come that they may have life, and that they may have it more abundantly."
John 10:10

Nicodemus needed to be born again
He needed LIFE

The Umbilical Cord Illustration

A baby in the womb is joined to the mother through the umbilical cord

  • The mother breathes for the baby
  • The mother inhales oxygen for the baby
  • The mother exhales carbon dioxide for the baby
  • The baby simply LETS the mother sustain its life

Sin Cut the Spiritual Umbilical Cord

We were created to be dependent on God

  • Sin entered and cut the spiritual umbilical cord
  • We were now dead in sin
  • We were dead to God
  • We were descending into eternal destruction

Emergency intervention was needed

God's Spiritual Surgery

  • On the cross, God performed spiritual surgery
  • He opened Jesus up
  • He restored the broken umbilical cord
  • He took us and placed us inside Jesus
  • He sealed the wound with the Holy Spirit

Now we are ONE with the Lord

Christ is Breathing for You

"You are alive because Jesus is inhaling. You are alive because Jesus is exhaling. And all you have to do is let Him."
Powerful Truth

Jesus Himself, by the Spirit of God,
is breathing for you spiritually

The Tea Bag Illustration

When you place a tea bag in water...

  • The water takes on the qualities of the tea
  • You now call it "tea"

God placed Christ in you and you in Christ
So that you become like Jesus

Not Imitation, But Participation

"Christianity is not imitation, it's participation in the very life of Christ."
Essential Truth

We are an aroma of Christ
Because He is in us and we are in Him

You ARE Dead to Sin

"The Bible doesn't say to the believer 'die to sin.' The Bible says to the believer, 'You ARE dead to sin, and you ARE alive to God.'"
Critical Truth

This is not a goal to achieve
This is an accomplished fact

Stop Trying to Die to Sin

"One of the reasons for your failure is you're trying to die to something you're already dead to."
Key Insight

You cannot die to something you're already dead to
The victory is already won

What About "I Die Daily"?

Paul was NOT talking about dying to sin

  • He was talking about daily persecution
  • Physical danger for the sake of the gospel
  • Constantly facing death threats
Read the context: 1 Corinthians 15:31

The Four Steps to Victory

Based on Romans 6:11-13

  • KNOW - Know you are dead to sin and alive to God
  • RECKON - Count it as unchangeable fact
  • LET NOT - Refuse to let sin reign
  • PRESENT - Offer yourself to God daily

Step 1: KNOW

"Knowing this, that our old man was crucified with Him, that the body of sin might be done away with, that we should no longer be slaves of sin."
Romans 6:6

You cannot walk in victory
until you KNOW you already have it

How to KNOW the Truth

  • Spend time in God's Word
  • Listen to sound teaching repeatedly
  • Write down key truths
  • Meditate on Scripture
  • Renew your mind daily

Satan's strategy is to keep you ignorant

Step 2: RECKON

"Likewise you also, reckon yourselves to be dead indeed to sin, but alive to God in Christ Jesus our Lord."
Romans 6:11

"Reckon" is an accounting term
It means count it as fact

Reckon It As Truth

  • It doesn't mean "pretend"
  • It means factor it into your thinking
  • Count on it as unchangeable truth
  • Believe what God says despite your feelings

Feelings can lie
God's Word never lies

Dead INDEED to Sin

"When someone is 'dead to you,' you have no interest in them, don't respond to their messages, walk past them as if they don't exist. That's how believers should be toward sin - dead to it indeed."
Powerful Analogy

Sin and I have nothing in common

Feelings vs. Faith

"Don't put your faith in feelings. Put your faith in the Word of God. Put your faith in the One who cannot lie."
Critical Truth
  • Feelings can lie to you
  • God's Word never changes
  • Believe what God says about you

Step 3: LET NOT

"Therefore do not let sin reign in your mortal body, that you should obey it in its lusts."
Romans 6:12

The only way sin can rule over you
is if you LET it

How to LET NOT

When temptation comes:

  • Say "NO" out loud
  • Declare: "I'm dead to sin, I'm alive to God"
  • Declare: "Christ is my life, I'm one with Him"
  • Declare: "The life of Christ in me empowers me to say no"

Faith works when you believe and speak

The Fight of Faith

"We fight the good fight of faith"
1 Timothy 6:12

The battle is not fighting sin
The battle is BELIEVING

You resist sin by believing the truth

Step 4: PRESENT

"Do not present your members as instruments of unrighteousness to sin, but present yourselves to God as being alive from the dead, and your members as instruments of righteousness to God."
Romans 6:13

Daily Prayer Practice

"Lord, this day I present my body to You to be an instrument for righteousness. I let You live Your life through me today, Christ. Bear Your fruit in me and through me, in Jesus' name."
Morning Declaration

Present yourself daily to God
as someone who is alive from the dead

Key Truth

"You are not a sinner trying to win victory over sin. You are a victor in Christ. The victory has already been won."
Identity in Christ

Stand, Don't Fight

"Satan will love for you to fight him and fight him and fight him. Because as long as you are fighting him, you are not walking in the victory Christ has already won."
Powerful Insight

Prayer Point 1

Union and Identification with Christ

"Or do you not know that as many of us as were baptized into Christ Jesus were baptized into His death? Therefore we were buried with Him through baptism into death, that just as Christ was raised from the dead by the glory of the Father, even so we also should walk in newness of life."
Romans 6:3-5

Thank God for your union with Christ. His story is your story: when He died, you died; when He rose, you rose; when He defeated sin, you defeated it in Him.

Prayer Point 2

Dead Indeed to Sin

"Likewise you also, reckon yourselves to be dead indeed to sin, but alive to God in Christ Jesus our Lord."
Romans 6:11

Declare: You are not trying to die to sin - you ARE dead to sin. This is an accomplished fact. Ask the Holy Spirit to renew your mind to this reality.

Prayer Point 3

Alive to God

"I have come that they may have life, and that they may have it more abundantly."
John 10:10

Thank God that you are ALIVE to God in Christ Jesus. The problem was death, but God's solution was life. Christ is your life.

Prayer Point 4

Christ Breathing Life Into You

"I have been crucified with Christ; it is no longer I who live, but Christ lives in me; and the life which I now live in the flesh I live by faith in the Son of God."
Galatians 2:20

Christ is literally breathing life into you spiritually. You simply let Him sustain you. Let Christ live His life through you today.

Prayer Point 5

Knowledge: The First Step to Victory

"Knowing this, that our old man was crucified with Him, that the body of sin might be done away with, that we should no longer be slaves of sin."
Romans 6:6

Commit to KNOWING the truth. Ask God for revelation knowledge of your position in Christ. Satan's strategy is to keep you ignorant.

This Week's Challenge

  • Listen to this sermon repeatedly until it becomes revelation
  • Memorize Romans 6:11
  • When tempted, speak aloud: "I'm dead to sin, I'm alive to God"
  • Daily prayer: Present your body as an instrument of righteousness
  • Stop trying to die to sin - believe you ARE dead to sin

Your Declaration

"I am crucified with Christ. I am dead to sin. I am alive to God. I have His life, His nature, and His ability working in me all the time. He's breathing in for me. He's exhaling out for me. And I am growing, maturing, being transformed into a perfect person, conformed to the image of Jesus. Thank you Lord. This is my reality. In Jesus' name."
Weekly Declaration

From This Day Forward

"From now on, don't fight FOR victory over sin, Satan, or self. From now on, stand IN the victory that Christ has won and let that victory manifest in your life by believing it, speaking it, declaring it, and renewing your mind with it."
Bishop Dr. Darlingston Johnson

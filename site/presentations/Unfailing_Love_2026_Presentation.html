Unfailing Love

Year of Unfailing Love 2026

"I have loved you with an everlasting love; therefore with loving kindness I have drawn you."
Jeremiah 31:3 (NKJV)
Bishop Dr. Darlingston Johnson
New Year's Eve Watchnight Service - December 31, 2025

God Speaks to His People

"The LORD has appeared of old to me, saying: 'Yes, I have loved you with an everlasting love; therefore with loving kindness I have drawn you.'"
Jeremiah 31:3 (NKJV)
God spoke these words to rebellious Israel - not words of rejection, but words of unfailing love. In 2026, hear God saying the same to YOU.

Settle This Issue Once and For All

  • God has loved you - not past tense, but continuous
  • His love is REGARDLESS - regardless of your failures and rebellion
  • If it wasn't regardless, none of us would be here tonight
  • Before God talks about obedience, He settles one issue: I HAVE LOVED YOU
  • This is the foundation for facing every test and trial in 2026
  • In the midst of every challenge, be grounded in this truth

God loves me with an everlasting love - REGARDLESS

The Nature of Everlasting Love

  • No beginning - God loved you before you were born
  • No end - No expiration date on His love
  • No vacation - His love never takes time off
  • Not based on your performance - it's not a roller coaster
  • Nothing you do can diminish it; nothing can increase it - it's already perfect
  • God loved you before you prayed the sinner's prayer
"God is love."
1 John 4:8 (NKJV)

Loving Kindness

A Love That ACTS

Loving Kindness - Covenant Love

  • Not just a passive sentiment or good feeling
  • Not just mental acceptance of you
  • A love that ACTS - it draws, gives, provides, protects
  • When God says "I love you," He means "I am going to take action"
  • God will never stand by passively - He will move on your behalf
  • In every fire, God doesn't just watch - He steps into it with you
"Don't go into 2026 saying there will be no fire. Go knowing God is going INTO the fire with you!"

God Cannot NOT Help You

  • God IS love (1 John 4:8) - it's His very nature
  • Love by definition acts, helps, and gives
  • God's love will not permit Him NOT to help you
  • God cannot cease to be God, therefore cannot cease to be love
  • Because He loves you with everlasting love, He MUST help you
  • The only thing that limits this love is our unbelief

"God, You cannot NOT help me. Your love is too strong. You are a prisoner of Your own love."

The Father's Unfailing Love

  • The prodigal son misbehaved, yet the father's love never changed
  • The father didn't wait for the son to prove himself
  • While the son was yet far away, the father RAN to embrace him
  • Dirty, smelling like pigs - it didn't matter to the father
  • The father loved him because that's who the father IS
  • God's love for us comes from GOD'S NATURE, not our behavior
"But when he was still a great way off, his father saw him and had compassion, and ran and fell on his neck and kissed him."
Luke 15:20 (NKJV)

More Than Conquerors

Romans 8:31-39

Nothing Will Separate You

  • If God is for us (loves us), who can be against us AND WIN?
  • Anything that comes against you must overcome God's love - IMPOSSIBLE
  • The love of God is the most powerful force in the universe
  • Death itself cannot separate you from God's love
  • Not angels, not principalities, not life, not death
  • Nothing in all creation can overpower the love of God at work in you
"What then shall we say to these things? If God is for us, who can be against us?"
Romans 8:31 (NKJV)

The Cross Proves His Love

  • Stop asking "Does God love me?" - Look at the cross!
  • God didn't just SAY it - He DEMONSTRATED it
  • Look at the nails - they say UNFAILING LOVE
  • Look at the crown of thorns - UNFAILING LOVE
  • Look at the blood Jesus shed - UNFAILING LOVE
  • The resurrection PROVES love has the power to overcome ANYTHING
"He who did not spare His own Son, but delivered Him up for us all, how shall He not with Him also freely give us all things?"
Romans 8:32 (NKJV)

What Love Has Already Done

  • God's love has already CALLED you - it cannot be undone
  • God's love has already JUSTIFIED you - your righteousness is secure
  • God's love has already GLORIFIED you - your destiny is sealed
  • Nothing in heaven or earth can change your position or status
  • The battle has already been fought; the victory already won
  • We don't fight FOR victory - we stand IN the victory already won

We are MORE than conquerors because LOVE has already conquered!

God's Love In Action - Testimony

  • The ministry needed $10,000 for the university gap
  • The Holy Spirit asked: "Who told you that YOU support ME?"
  • Bishop stopped worrying and started thanking God
  • A young member unexpectedly gave an envelope - $20,000!
  • God's love is concrete, practical, substantial, evidential
  • This kind of love will be working in YOUR life in 2026
"When you rely on His love, know His love, trust His love - that's the kind of love you'll experience in 2026!"

Your love never takes a vacation. Nothing I do can diminish it. I will not be afraid of tests, trials, or the enemy because Your love is the most powerful force in the universe. I trust that You cannot NOT help me because You cannot cease to be love. In every challenge, I expect Your loving kindness to move on my behalf.
Jeremiah 31:3; 1 John 4:8, 16

Nothing can separate me from Your love. Nothing can undo what Your love has done. I've been called, justified, and glorified - no power can change my status.

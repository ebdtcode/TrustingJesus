When God's Life Becomes Yours

"And this is the testimony: that God has given us eternal life, and this life is in His Son."
1 John 5:11
Bishop Dr. Darlingston Johnson
God's Unfailing Love Series - Part 4
Harvest Intercontinental Church - Olney

The Gap Between Knowledge and Living

  • There's a gap between how we live and how we know we should live
  • We hear commands: "Love your enemies," "Forgive one another"
  • We try like New Year's resolutions - and often fail
  • This leads to condemnation, guilt, and frustration
"We often hear the command to love without understanding why we can and how it becomes possible"

The Answer

Eternal Life - The Capacity to Love

Two Kinds of Life

Bios (Biological Life)

  • Natural life all humans possess
  • Energy source for the physical body
  • Like gasoline for a car
  • Subject to decay and death
  • Scientists understand this life

Zoe (Eternal Life)

  • The God-kind of life
  • Energy source for your spirit
  • Like jet fuel for an airplane
  • Not subject to death or decay
  • Scientists know nothing of this

What Is Eternal Life?

  • Zoe is the source of energy for your spirit to function
  • Just as biological life enables your body, eternal life enables your spirit
  • It causes your spirit to function as God designed - to love, believe, and obey
  • Eternal life is not something you get after you die - you have it NOW
"He who has the Son has life; he who does not have the Son of God does not have life."
1 John 5:12

A Present Possession, Not Future Hope

"These things I have written to you who believe in the name of the Son of God, that you may know that you have eternal life."
1 John 5:13
  • John wrote so you may KNOW - not just repeat information
  • Heaven is in the future; eternal life is in you NOW
  • If you say "I will have eternal life" - you don't know this truth
  • Right now, you HAVE the capacity to love as Christ loves

Christ Is Your Life

The Source of Everything

Christ Who Is Our Life

"For you died, and your life is hidden with Christ in God. When Christ who is our life appears, then you also will appear with Him in glory."
Colossians 3:3-4
  • Eternal life is not just a gift FROM Christ - it IS Christ in you
  • Jesus is not just the giver of life, He IS the life itself
  • You and He are one spirit - He is joined to you
  • His life is supplying the love energy you need

God Doesn't Improve - He Replaces

  • God's purpose is NOT to empower you to lead your own life
  • God does not do "fixer uppers" with your old nature
  • He puts your old life to death and throws it away
  • He replaces your life with His own divine life
"I have been crucified with Christ; it is no longer I who live, but Christ lives in me."
Galatians 2:20

Christianity Is Participating in God's Life

Not Religious Rules

Christianity is not trying to obey laws through human effort.
That would make it no different from any other religion.

"I'm a Christian because the life of God - eternal life - is dwelling in my spirit, empowering me to be all God has called me to be"

The Law of the Spirit of Life

"For the law of the Spirit of life in Christ Jesus has made me free from the law of sin and death."
Romans 8:2
  • The law of sin operates with biological life - pulling you to selfishness
  • The law of the Spirit of life is a higher law
  • Like aerodynamics overcomes gravity making airplanes fly
  • Eternal life transcends and overcomes the flesh, enabling you to walk in love

Security in God's Love

"As the Father loved Me, I also have loved you; abide in My love."
John 15:9
  • The Father loves Jesus no more than Jesus loves you
  • Abide in this revelation - don't move one inch from it
  • When you know how much you are loved, you become the most secure person
  • When secure in love, you don't need to defend yourself - you're free to love others

Eternal Life: God's Answer to the Enemy

"The thief comes only to steal and kill and destroy. I came that they may have life and have it abundantly."
John 10:10
  • Satan's agenda: steal, kill, destroy
  • Jesus came to give you eternal life - in abundance
  • Eternal life is God's answer to every attack of the enemy
  • When the devil meets eternal life... victory is assured!

Prayer Focus

Revelation of Eternal Life

"Father, open our eyes to see that eternal life is not a future hope but a present possession. Your life - the life of Christ - dwells in our spirits right now. Break every deception that says we will receive eternal life someday. Let us know that we know that we have the very life of God empowering us to love as Christ loves."
Based on 1 John 5:11-13

Prayer Focus

Walking in the Capacity to Love

"Lord Jesus, we thank You that the capacity to love is already in us through eternal life. We repent of trying to manufacture love through willpower. Energize our spirits with Your love. Let the love of God shed abroad in our hearts flow freely to others. Help us abide in Your love so secure that we love freely without defensiveness or fear."
Based on Romans 5:5; John 15:9; Galatians 2:20

The Key to Walking in Love

Revelation and Reliance

  • Revelation - Understand what eternal life is and what you possess
  • Reliance - Learn to depend on it constantly, not your own strength
  • The capacity is there. The love is there. The power is there.
  • Meditate on this truth until it moves from head to heart
"This life that I have is the life of God in me"

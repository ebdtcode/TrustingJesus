Our Foundation Scripture

"There was a man of the Pharisees named Nicodemus, a ruler of the Jews. This man came to Jesus by night and said to Him, 'Rabbi, we know that You are a teacher come from God; for no one can do these signs that You do unless God is with him.' Jesus answered and said to him, 'Most assuredly, I say to you, unless one is born again, he cannot see the kingdom of God.'"
John 3:1-3

The Problem: Spiritual Death from Adam

  • Sin entered through one man - Adam
  • Death spread to all mankind
  • We are born sinners, spiritually dead
  • Religious knowledge cannot fix this
  • Man's problem is not more teaching - man needs LIFE
"Through one man sin entered the world, and death through sin, and thus death spread to all men, because all sinned."
Romans 5:12

The Natural Man Cannot Understand Spiritual Things

"But the natural man does not receive the things of the Spirit of God, for they are foolishness to him; nor can he know them, because they are spiritually discerned."
1 Corinthians 2:14

Kuku-jumuku: If you're not inside, you can't know!

The Solution: Born Again Through God's Word

"Having been born again, not of corruptible seed but incorruptible, through the word of God which lives and abides forever."
1 Peter 1:23
  • God's Word is the incorruptible seed
  • It enters through our ears into our spirit
  • We receive divine, eternal life - not Adamic life

The Kingdom of God: Righteousness, Peace & Joy

"For the kingdom of God is not eating and drinking, but righteousness and peace and joy in the Holy Spirit."
Romans 14:17
  • Not heaven only - a present reality within you
  • Righteousness from God, not your own works
  • Supernatural peace and joy in the Spirit

The Righteous Are Bold as a Lion

"The wicked flee when no one pursues, but the righteous are bold as a lion."
Proverbs 28:1
  • Backed by God's authority
  • Effective, fervent prayer that avails much
  • The Lord hears the prayers of the righteous
  • Signs and wonders follow believers

Spirit-Led Living as Sons of God

"For as many as are led by the Spirit of God, these are sons of God."
Romans 8:14
  • Divine guidance in daily matters
  • Creative wisdom and supernatural ideas
  • Provision that defies natural explanation
  • Walking by faith, not by sight

The Choice: Adam or Christ

"I have been crucified with Christ; it is no longer I who live, but Christ lives in me; and the life which I now live in the flesh I live by faith in the Son of God, who loved me and gave Himself for me."
Galatians 2:20

I choose to be in Jesus. I refuse to be in Adam.

Prayer for Salvation and New Birth

Lord Jesus, I acknowledge that I cannot comprehend spiritual things in my natural state. I confess that I am born in sin through Adam's lineage, spiritually dead and unable to save myself. Today I receive Your incorruptible seed, the Word of God, into my spirit. I believe Jesus Christ died for my sins and rose again. I renounce my old Adamic nature and choose to be in Christ. Make me a new creation. Give me Your divine life, that I may know You and walk in Your kingdom. Save me now, in Jesus's name.

Prayer for Walking in Imputed Righteousness

Father, I thank You that through Christ, I have become the righteousness of God. My own righteousness is as filthy rags, but Christ's righteousness has been imputed to me. The righteous are bold as a lion - I receive this boldness to pray, to witness, and to live victoriously. I refuse condemnation because there is no condemnation for those in Christ Jesus. Let me walk in the confidence that You hear my prayers and that I have access to Your throne of grace.

Prayer for Resurrection Power Over Dead Situations

Lord, just as Jesus called Lazarus from the grave, I call forth resurrection life over every dead situation in my life. You are the God who calls those things that are not as though they were. I speak life to dead relationships, dead dreams, dead businesses, and dead hopes. Greater is He who is in me than he who is in the world. I refuse to accept death where You have promised life. Let Your resurrection power that raised Christ from the dead quicken every area of my life.

This Week's Challenge

  • Make a conscious decision today: Am I truly born again?
  • Walk in your authority as a righteous believer
  • Listen for the Holy Spirit's guidance in daily matters
  • Speak life to dead situations around you
  • Boldly testify of the new life you have in Christ

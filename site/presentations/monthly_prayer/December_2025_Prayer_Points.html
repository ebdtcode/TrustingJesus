    Prayer Points - December 3-4, 2025

    Theme: Year-End Thanksgiving and Preparation

    • Two days of focused prayer
    • Day 1: December 3 - Thanksgiving, Intercession, and Unity
    • Day 2: December 4 - Divine Direction, Harvest, and Victory
    • Year of Unshakable Hope

    Day 1: December 3, 2025

    Thanksgiving, Intercession, and Unity

    Opening Focus:

    • Thanksgiving for God's Faithfulness and Divine Protection
    • Intercession for Leadership
    • Prayers for Emerging Leaders
    • Alignment, Unity, and New Identity in Christ

    1. Thanksgiving for Faithfulness and Divine Protection

    "The steadfast love of the Lord never ceases; his mercies never come to an end; they are new every morning; great is your faithfulness."

    Lamentations 3:22-23

    Thanksgiving Prayer Points

    Pray:

    • Thank God for His unwavering faithfulness toward Harvest Olney and for His mercies that greet us anew each morning
    • Thank the Lord for His hand of preservation over our lives this year, shielding us from seen and unseen dangers and delivering us from every scheme of the enemy
    • Give Him praise for sustaining us spiritually, physically, and emotionally from the beginning of the year until now

    2. Intercession for the Bishop and Pastors

    "Then fear came upon every soul, and many wonders and signs were done through the apostles."

    Acts 2:43

    Prayer Points for Leadership

    Pray:

    • Ask the Lord to continually grant our Bishop and Pastors fresh insight and deep revelation regarding the fundamentals of the Kingdom
    • Pray for their total well-being; ask God to preserve their health, grant them soundness of mind, and renew their strength like the eagle's
    • Declare that a fresh Spirit of boldness rests upon them to stand firm for the Kingdom and proclaim the Word of God with power

    3. Prayers for End-of-Year Programs

    "And suddenly there came a sound from heaven, as of a rushing mighty wind, and it filled the whole house where they were sitting."

    Acts 2:2

    End-of-Year Programs Prayer Points

    Pray:

    • Pray for an unprecedented move of the Holy Spirit during our end-of-year gatherings at Harvest Olney
    • Ask for a release of grace to tarry in His presence, experiencing Him in a fresh and new way during these events
    • Pray for divine encounters and the salvation of the lost throughout our year-end activities

    4. Prayer for Emerging Leaders

    "But the manifestation of the Spirit is given to each one for the profit of all."

    1 Corinthians 12:7

    Emerging Leaders Prayer Points

    Pray:

    • Pray that every member of the Harvest family discovers and cultivates the specific gifts the Holy Spirit has placed within them
    • Lift up emerging leaders, asking that they receive the boldness required to answer the call to follow Christ and lead others effectively
    • Ask the Holy Spirit to ignite a new zeal and a deepened commitment to the Lord within us like never before

    5. Prayer for Alignment and Unity

    "Fulfill my joy by being like-minded, having the same love, being of one accord, of one mind."

    Philippians 2:2

    Alignment and Unity Prayer Points

    Pray:

    • Decree that all Harvesters are in perfect accord with the vision and mission of the House
    • Pray for hearts that are willing to respond rightly to God's direction and remain aligned with our leadership
    • Declare that our bond of unity is strengthened; we rebuke every form of division or disunity in our midst

    6. Thanksgiving for Provision

    "And my God shall supply all your need according to His riches in glory by Christ Jesus."

    Philippians 4:19

    Provision Prayer Points

    Pray:

    • Acknowledge God as our Jehovah Jireh, thanking Him for His consistent provision throughout the year
    • Thank Him for opening doors of opportunity and keeping us from lack
    • Declare that every need is met; we lack nothing and walk in an abundance of blessings

    7. Declaration of Our New Identity

    "In Him we have redemption through His blood, the forgiveness of sins, according to the riches of His grace."

    Ephesians 1:7

    New Identity Prayer Points

    Pray:

    • Give thanks for the finished work of Christ and the precious gift of salvation
    • Declare that we are new creations; the old has passed away, and all things have become new
    • Pray for a deepened revelation that we are holy, blameless, and fully accepted in the Beloved

    Day 2: December 4, 2025

    Divine Direction, Harvest, and Victory

    Opening Focus:

    • Divine Direction for the New Service
    • Successful Planning and Resources
    • End-of-Year Harvest of Souls
    • Miracles, Testimonies, and Victory

    1. Prayer for Divine Direction (New Service)

    "Your ears shall hear a word behind you, saying, 'This is the way, walk in it'..."

    Isaiah 30:21

    Divine Direction Prayer Points

    Pray:

    • Ask God to open our spiritual ears to hear His specific instructions regarding the planning of the new service
    • Pray for clarity of voice - that we would hear Him distinctively saying, "This is the way, walk in it"
    • Ask that divine guidance becomes the standard for our lives as we navigate this season of expansion

    2. Prayer for Successful Planning

    "Unless the Lord builds the house, those who build it labor in vain."

    Psalm 127:1

    Successful Planning Prayer Points

    Pray:

    • Thank the Lord for providing the necessary resources to start and sustain our new service plans
    • Praise Him for the wisdom and expertise granted to everyone involved in the strategy and execution
    • Pray for the successful launch of this service, that it may bring glory to His name

    3. Prayer for End-of-Year Harvest

    "And the Lord added to the church daily those who were being saved."

    Acts 2:47

    End-of-Year Harvest Prayer Points

    Pray:

    • Prophesy that the Lord is bringing a "tsunami of souls" to Harvest Olney during our end-of-year programs
    • Declare that this harvest includes people from all nations and backgrounds - Hispanics, Caucasians, Africans, African-Americans, Europeans, Asians, and Arabs
    • Cover our harvester brothers/sisters and family members in the blood of Jesus, praying for conviction of heart and the boldness to follow Christ

    4. Prayer for Fresh Anointing on Leadership

    "But the path of the just (righteous) is like the light of dawn, that shines brighter and brighter..."

    Proverbs 4:18

    Fresh Anointing Prayer Points

    Pray:

    • Pray that our Bishop and Pastors increase in divine knowledge and revelation as they walk closely with God
    • Declare that the Lord visits the church with a fresh power to demonstrate His Kingdom in public places
    • Pray that all glory belongs to God for the work the Holy Spirit is doing through our leaders

    5. Prayer for Miracles and Testimonies

    "And He said, 'My Presence will go with you, and I will give you rest.'"

    Exodus 33:14

    Miracles and Testimonies Prayer Points

    Pray:

    • Ask the Holy Spirit to grant every Harvester undeniable testimonies of God's goodness in all areas of life before the year ends
    • Thank Him for manifesting healing and restoring strength
    • Declare that we are visited repeatedly with miracles, signs, and wonders

    6. Declaration of Dominion and Victory

    "If then you were raised with Christ, seek those things which are above, where Christ is, sitting at the right hand of God. Set your mind on things above, not on things on the earth."

    Colossians 3:1-2

    Dominion and Victory Prayer Points

    Pray:

    • Pray that we walk in the reality that we have been raised with Christ, living the "Higher Life" that is distinct from the patterns of this world
    • Declare that we set our minds on things above; our perspective is elevated, and we operate from a position of victory rather than defeat
    • Decree that because we are seated with Christ at the right hand of God, we function far above every earthly limitation and spiritual opposition

    7. Thanksgiving for Growth and Learning

    "And Jesus increased in wisdom and stature, and in favor with God and men."

    Luke 2:52

    Growth and Learning Prayer Points

    Pray:

    • Thank the Lord for the wisdom gained through this year's journey and the lessons learned in challenging times
    • Praise Him for the growth we have experienced in our relationship with Him
    • Declare that we continue to increase in wisdom, stature, and favor with God and men

    Closing

    Thank God:

    For the assurance that as we, the righteous, have prayed in agreement in the name of Jesus according to the Word of God, He has heard and answered. Amen.

    Year of Unshakable Hope

    • Our hope is firm and secure in Christ
    • Our prayers are heard and answered
    • God is faithful to His promises
    • We finish this year with unshakable hope and enter the new year with expectation

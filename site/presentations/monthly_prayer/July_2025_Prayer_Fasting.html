    Day 1: Personal Consecration

    July 3, 2025

    "I beseech you therefore, brethren, by the mercies of God, that ye present your bodies a living sacrifice, holy, acceptable unto God, which is your reasonable service."

    Romans 12:1 (KJV)

    Personal Holiness

    "Follow peace with all men, and holiness, without which no man shall see the Lord."

    Hebrews 12:14 (KJV)

    • Pray for grace to walk in holiness daily
    • Deliverance from every secret sin and struggle
    • Strength to resist temptation and worldliness
    • Purity of heart, mind, and body
    • Christlike character development

    Spiritual Disciplines

    "But his delight is in the law of the LORD; and in his law doth he meditate day and night."

    Psalm 1:2 (KJV)

    • Pray for consistency in Bible reading and study
    • Deeper prayer life and communion with God
    • Effective fasting and spiritual exercises
    • Worship and praise as lifestyle
    • Regular times of silence and meditation

    Spiritual Gifts Activation

    "Neglect not the gift that is in thee, which was given thee by prophecy, with the laying on of the hands of the presbytery."

    1 Timothy 4:14 (KJV)

    • Pray for discovery of spiritual gifts
    • Activation and manifestation of gifts
    • Boldness to operate in the gifts
    • Wisdom in the use of spiritual gifts
    • Multiplication and increase of gifts

    Intimacy with God

    "As the hart panteth after the water brooks, so panteth my soul after thee, O God."

    Psalm 42:1 (KJV)

    • Pray for deeper hunger and thirst for God
    • Personal encounters and experiences with God
    • Sensitivity to the Holy Spirit's voice
    • Surrender to God's will completely
    • Love relationship that surpasses knowledge

    Personal Deliverance

    "If the Son therefore shall make you free, ye shall be free indeed."

    John 8:36 (KJV)

    • Pray for freedom from every bondage and addiction
    • Breaking of generational curses and patterns
    • Deliverance from fear, worry, and anxiety
    • Freedom from past hurts and traumas
    • Complete liberation in spirit, soul, and body

    Day 1 Declarations

    • I am a new creation in Christ Jesus
    • Old things have passed away, all things are new
    • I am dead to sin and alive unto God
    • My body is the temple of the Holy Spirit
    • I walk in holiness and righteousness daily
    • I am being transformed into Christ's image
    • I am free from every bondage and limitation

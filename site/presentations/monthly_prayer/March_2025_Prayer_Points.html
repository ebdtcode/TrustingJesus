    2 Days of Prayer

    March 5-6, 2025

    Celebrating 25 Years of the Bishopric

    Prayer Focus

    • Day 1: Praying for HIM-U and the Nations
    • Day 2: Praying for Harvest Olney
    • Special focus: 25th Bishopric Celebration

    Day 1 - March 5, 2025

    Praying for HIM-U and the Nations

    "He who dwells in the shelter of the Most High will remain secure and rest in the shadow of the Almighty [whose power no enemy can withstand]."

    Psalm 91:1 (AMP)

    Dwelling in God's Shelter

    "He who dwells in the shelter of the Most High will remain secure and rest in the shadow of the Almighty [whose power no enemy can withstand]."

    Psalm 91:1 (AMP)

    • Thank God that HIM-U will continually dwell under His shelter
    • Thank God that HIM-U's security and confidence is in Jesus and Him alone

    25 Years of Faithfulness

    "It is good to give thanks to the LORD, and to sing praises to Your name, O Most High; to declare Your lovingkindness in the morning, and Your faithfulness every night."

    Psalm 92:1-2

    • Thank God, praise his Holy Name as HIM-U celebrates 25 years of the Bishopric
    • Thank God for His consistent lovingkindness and faithfulness to Bishop and Lady Chrys
    • Declare that HIM-U will fulfill her mission of winning the lost at all costs

    Shepherds After God's Heart

    "And I will give you shepherds according to My heart, who will feed you with knowledge and understanding."

    Jeremiah 3:15

    "The eyes of your understanding being enlightened; that you may know what is the hope of His calling, what are the riches of the glory of His inheritance in the saints."

    Ephesians 1:18

    Prayer for Bishop and Lady Chrys

    • Thank God for Bishop and Lady Chrys who obeyed the Lord to become missionaries and not refugees
    • Thank God for their faithfulness as committed Shepherds of HIM-U
    • Pray that God continues to enlighten their eyes of understanding in their knowledge of Him spiritually and experientially
    • Pray that the Spirit of the Lord will rest on them mightily as they lead HIM-U
    • Pray they lack nothing good spiritually, emotionally, physically, and financially

    Salt of the Earth

    "You are the salt of the earth; but if the salt has lost its taste (purpose), how can it be made salty? It is no longer good for anything, but to be thrown out and walked on by people."

    Matthew 5:13 (AMP)

    HIM-U: High Quality Salt

    • Thank God for making HIM-U churches the salt in every locality where He planted us
    • Pray that HIM-U churches will be a high-quality salt in the hand of God
    • Pray that no conditions or cultures will render HIM-U churches powerless or ineffective

    Prayer for Leaders

    "I urge, then, first of all, that petitions, prayers, intercession and thanksgiving be made for all people‚Äîfor kings and all those in authority, that we may live peaceful and quiet lives in all godliness and holiness."

    1 Timothy 2:1-2 (NIV)

    "When the righteous are in authority, the people rejoice; but when a wicked man rules, the people groan."

    Proverbs 29:2 (NKJV)

    Wisdom for Leaders

    • Pray for the President, Vice President, members of Congress and State officials that they will lead by the wisdom of God
    • Pray that the Lord will give them a heart of compassion to lead the people
    • Pray that the Lord will cause righteous men and women to have the ear of the leaders who will hear godly counsel

    Believers in Position of Power

    "One day as Mordecai was on duty at the king's gate, two of the king's eunuchs, Bigthana and Teresh‚Äîwho were guards at the door of the king's private quarters‚Äîbecame angry at King Xerxes and plotted to assassinate him."

    Esther 2:21 (NIV)

    • Pray for believers in position of power to hear what the Lord is saying concerning their nations and act accordingly
    • Pray that those in position of power seeking to block the work of God be removed

    Lifting Up the Hands

    "But Moses' hands became heavy; so they took a stone and put it under him, and he sat on it. And Aaron and Hur supported his hands, one on one side, and the other on the other side; and his hands were steady until the going down of the sun. So Joshua defeated Amalek and his people with the edge of the sword."

    Exodus 17:12-13

    Prayer for HIM-U Bishops

    • Thank God for Bishop Musa Korfeh, Bishop Bouerima Kimso, Bishop Julius Lagaah, Bishop Daniel Njoya, Bishop Victor Okofor, Bishop Wolo Belleh, and their First Ladies
    • Thank God for those who are lifting up the hands of Bishop Johnson and Lady Chrys
    • Pray that they continuously trust in the Lord and lean on His understanding as they serve HIM-U
    • Declare that they prosper and be in good health even as their souls prosper

    Provision for Travel

    "And my God shall supply all your need according to His riches in glory by Christ Jesus."

    Philippians 4:19

    • Agree with the Word of God that those needing Visas will receive them
    • None will lack travel and accommodation finances whether they travel internationally or locally
    • Declare all modes of transportation are covered by the blood of Jesus
    • Cancel incidents and accidents as people come and go back to their respective destinations

    Prayer for Peace

    "And work for the peace and prosperity of the city where I sent you into exile. Pray to the LORD for it, for its welfare will determine your welfare."

    Jeremiah 29:7 (NLT)

    • Taking DRC as a point of contact, pray for the peace of nations affected by war
    • Pray for the leaders to receive godly wisdom to put an end to the conflicts

    Day 2 - March 6, 2025

    Praying for Harvest Olney

    "He lets me lie down in green pastures; He leads me beside the still and quiet waters."

    Psalm 23:2 (AMP)

    The Word Preached

    "He lets me lie down in green pastures; He leads me beside the still and quiet waters."

    Psalm 23:2 (AMP)

    • Declare that the word of God preached in Harvest Olney will cause the hearers to be stabilized in their faith
    • Thank God that the word preached in Harvest Olney will refresh our souls and manifest Christ in and through us

    Breaking Through Spiritually

    "Listen. No one is able to break into a mighty man's house and steal his property unless he first overpowers the mighty man and ties him up. Then his entire house can be plundered and his possessions taken."

    Mark 3:27 (TPT)

    "Every place on which the sole of your foot treads shall become yours; your territory shall be from the wilderness to Lebanon, and from the river, the river Euphrates, as far as the western sea (the Mediterranean)."

    Deuteronomy 11:24 (AMP)

    Taking Territory for God

    • Declare that the spiritual climate over Olney and surrounding cities is conducive for people being saved in their numbers
    • Pray that any mighty man ruling over Olney and surrounding cities is disposed in Jesus name
    • Thank God that Olney and surrounding cities belong to our God and His Christ
    • Thank God that Harvest Olney is one of the churches God is using to bring great salvation to Olney and surrounding cities

    Ministry Workers

    "Work willingly at whatever you do, as though you were working for the Lord rather than for people. Remember that the Lord will give you an inheritance as your reward, and that the Master you are serving is Christ."

    Colossians 3:23-24 (NLT)

    • Thank God for all the Ministry Workers of Harvest Olney as they give themselves to serve and go the extra mile during The Bishopric
    • Pray for unity of purpose and of mind, peace and strength
    • Thank God for rewarding them in this life and the one to come

    Bishopric Celebration Services

    "And my speech and my preaching were not with persuasive words of human wisdom, but in demonstration of the Spirit and of power, that your faith should not be in the wisdom of men but in the power of God."

    1 Corinthians 2:4-5

    Manifested Presence of God

    • Pray for the Bishopric Celebration for the manifested presence of God during the evening services and teaching sessions during the day
    • Pray that the Word will be preached in the demonstration of the Spirit and of power
    • Pray a glory cloud to tabernacle with us during Praise and Worship Sessions

    Power to Get Wealth

    "And you shall remember the LORD your God, for it is He who gives you power to get wealth, that He may establish His covenant which He swore to your fathers, as it is this day."

    Deuteronomy 8:18 (NIV)

    Prospering for God's Glory

    • Thank God for empowering Harvesters (better paying jobs, promotions, raises, flourishing businesses, ideas, business relations) to be prosperous for His Glory
    • Pray that God is empowering us to be debt free so we have more resources for every good work
    • Declare that as God prospers us we will testify of His faithfulness and remain humble

    Cheerful and Generous Giving

    "Remember this: Whoever sows sparingly will also reap sparingly, and whoever sows generously will also reap generously. Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver."

    2 Corinthians 9:6-7 (NIV)

    • Pray that every Harvester will reflect the heart of our Father through our life of generosity
    • Pray that every Harvester will be both willing and able to contribute to "Raising the Walls"

    Marriage and Relationships

    "However, each man among you [without exception] is to love his wife as his very own self [with behavior worthy of respect and esteem, always seeking the best for her with an attitude of lovingkindness], and the wife [must see to it] that she respects and delights in her husband."

    Ephesians 5:33 (AMP)

    • Pray that marriages in Harvest will blossom based on love and mutual respect
    • Pray that the Lord will restore relationships (marriages, between parents and children, among brethren) in Harvest Olney

    A Praying Church

    "Also [Jesus] told them a parable to the effect that they ought always to pray and not to turn coward (faint, lose heart, and give up)."

    Luke 18:1 (AMPC)

    • Thank God for making Harvest Olney a Bible based praying church
    • Thank God for the grace to wait on Him in prayer until we receive the answer

    Employment and Provision

    "Go around and ask all your neighbors for empty jars. Don't ask for just a few. Then go inside and shut the door behind you and your sons. Pour oil into all the jars, and as each is filled, put it to one side... Go, sell the oil and pay your debts. You and your sons can live on what is left."

    2 Kings 4:1-7 (NIV)

    Supernatural Provision

    • Pray that God will comfort our brethren who lost their jobs
    • Thank God for supernatural provisions for our brethren who are in between jobs
    • Pray that God will favor our brethren as they apply for jobs and are interviewed
    • Pray that the Holy Spirit will inspire witty ideas to our brethren to start successful businesses

    Prayer for Immigrants

    "Every place on which the sole of your foot treads shall become yours; your territory shall be from the wilderness to Lebanon, and from the river, the river Euphrates, as far as the western sea (the Mediterranean)."

    Deuteronomy 11:24 (AMP)

    "I now give you permission to make a law that will save the lives of your people. You may use my ring to seal the law, so that it can never be changed."

    Esther 8:7 (CEV)

    America as Our Home

    • Thank God for giving us America as our home
    • Pray to reverse every evil law targeting immigrants in this country
    • Pray for laws that enable the situation of undocumented immigrants to be regularized

    By His Stripes We Are Healed

    "But He was wounded for our transgressions, He was crushed for our wickedness [our sin, our injustice, our wrongdoing]; The punishment [required] for our well-being fell on Him, and by His stripes (wounds) we are healed."

    Isaiah 53:5 (AMP)

    Enjoying Divine Health

    • Thank Jesus for paying for our healing in full and that no disease can withstand Him
    • Thank Jesus for being our personal healer (the Great Physician)
    • Pray to enforce the healing that we graciously received through Jesus
    • Declare that we are enjoying divine health

    Closing Prayer

    Thank God for 25 years of faithful leadership and ministry through the Bishopric. As we have prayed in agreement according to the Word of God, we declare that He has heard and answered our prayers.

    Amen

    Declaration: Psalm 91 (Paraphrased)

    The hand of the Lord is upon us. No weapon formed against us shall prosper.

    God, you're our refuge. We trust in you and we are safe! You rescue us from hidden traps, shield us from deadly hazards. Your huge, outstretched arms protect us — under them we are perfectly safe.

    We will fear nothing—not wild wolves in the night, not flying arrows in the day, not disease that prowls through the darkness, not disaster that erupts at high noon.

    Even though others succumb all around, drop like flies right and left, no harm will even graze us. We will stand untouched, watch it all from a distance. Yes, because God is our refuge.

    Evil can't get close to us, harm can't get through the door. He ordered his angels to guard us wherever we are.

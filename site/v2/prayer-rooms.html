Front Door

Protection & Welcome

"The Lord will watch over your coming and going both now and forevermore."

Psalm 121:8 (NIV) Read on Bible.com →

"When you enter a house, first say, 'Peace to this house.'"

Luke 10:5 (NIV) Read on Bible.com →

Prayer

Lord, we dedicate this entrance to You. May everyone who enters this home feel Your peace and presence. Protect all who come and go. Let this doorway be a threshold of blessing, where Your angels stand guard and Your love welcomes all.

🛋

Living Room

Family Unity & Worship

"But as for me and my household, we will serve the Lord."

Joshua 24:15 (NIV) Read on Bible.com →

"How good and pleasant it is when God's people live together in unity!"

Psalm 133:1 (NIV) Read on Bible.com →

Prayer

Father, let this room be a place of unity, love, and joy. May our family gather here in peace, sharing laughter and building memories that honor You. Let this space be filled with Your presence, where we worship together and grow in faith.

🍽

Dining Room

Provision & Gratitude

"You prepare a table before me in the presence of my enemies. You anoint my head with oil; my cup overflows."

Psalm 23:5 (NIV) Read on Bible.com →

"Give us today our daily bread."

Matthew 6:11 (NIV) Read on Bible.com →

Prayer

Lord, we thank You for Your faithful provision. Bless this table where we gather to nourish our bodies. May our meals be times of gratitude and fellowship. Let every conversation here build up and encourage. Thank You for being Jehovah Jireh, our Provider.

🍳

Kitchen

Nourishment & Service

"She gets up while it is still night; she provides food for her family."

Proverbs 31:15 (NIV) Read on Bible.com →

"Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart, as working for the Lord."

Colossians 3:23 (NIV) Read on Bible.com →

Prayer

Heavenly Father, bless the hands that prepare meals in this kitchen. May every dish be made with love and gratitude. Let this space be a place of joyful service, where we care for our family's needs. Fill our home with the aroma of Your love and provision.

🛌

Bedroom

Rest & Peace

"In peace I will lie down and sleep, for you alone, Lord, make me dwell in safety."

Psalm 4:8 (NIV) Read on Bible.com →

"He who dwells in the shelter of the Most High will rest in the shadow of the Almighty."

Psalm 91:1 (NIV) Read full Psalm →

Prayer

Lord, we consecrate this room for rest and renewal. Guard our sleep and fill our dreams with Your peace. May we wake refreshed and ready to serve You. Let this bedroom be a sanctuary where we find comfort in Your presence and strength for each new day.

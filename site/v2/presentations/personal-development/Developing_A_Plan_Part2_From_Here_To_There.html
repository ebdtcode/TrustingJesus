Four Steps to Get There

From 1 Kings 17:3-4 (Elijah at Brook Cherith)

1. Get Away
2. Turn
3. Hide
4. Drink
Step 1

Get Away - Make A Move

"You can't move from here to there by staying here. You have to get away. You have to take a step."

"One small step for a man. One giant leap for mankind."

  • Talk to that person
  • Register for that course
  • Apply for that opportunity
  • Take that test
Step 2

Turn - Change Direction

"What people usually call generational curses are simply people repeating the same choices and patterns. It's just repetition."

  • Break family dysfunction patterns
  • Choose different models
  • Establish new patterns
  • Live differently from the example you saw
Step 3

Hide - Set Yourself Apart

"Many of the important things we have to do must be done alone. The things that really change our lives are done privately."

  • Work on character when no one is watching
  • Pray intimately with God
  • Seek God's approval over people's applause

"Progress is not a popular thing"

Step 4

Drink - Receive Fresh Input

"You're going to expose yourself to new knowledge, new information, new relationships, new concepts."

  • New books and learning
  • Different perspectives
  • Digital/tech skills
  • Diverse relationships

The world is changing. Stay current or get left behind.

The Power of Human Innovation

"Almost every situation can be overcome in life. Almost every situation can be overcome."

  • Humans learned to fly (once impossible)
  • From no phones to video calls across continents
  • From office jobs to remote work worldwide

What seems impossible today becomes normal tomorrow

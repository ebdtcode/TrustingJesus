The Core Truth

"Becoming is winning. The kind of person who wins is the person you choose to become."

  • What you put on paper won't happen by itself
  • YOU make things happen
  • Success depends on who you become, not just what you write

The Principle of Chosen Slavery

"Everybody is a slave to something. You must choose what kind of slave and whose slave you want to be."

Warning

Destructive Slaveries

What most people are enslaved to:

  • Sin
  • Food (specific foods, sugar, carbohydrates)
  • Entertainment
  • Spending / Shopping
  • Comfort and complacency

"You know you are a slave when you are doing something you know is not good for you but you keep doing it."

Choose These

Constructive Slaveries

Slave to Christ
Slave to the Holy Spirit
Slave to the Word of God
Slave to righteousness
Slave to integrity
Slave to honor
Slave to wisdom
Slave to learning
Slave to quality
Slave to excellence

The Self-Marking Principle

"You get to a point in life where nobody marks your paper for you. You mark your own paper."

  • You must choose your own slavery
  • You must set your own standards
  • You must push yourself

Application

"I study because I am a slave to learning. I'm a slave to quality. I'm a slave to excellence."

What Winning Really Means

Definition 1

Become the person God wants me to be

Definition 2

Do the things God wants me to do

"You can be famous and miserable. The greatest achievement is not what you get - it's becoming and doing what God wants for your life."

Redefining Achievement

Wrong Measurement

  • Money accumulated
  • Buildings constructed
  • Fame achieved
  • Material possessions

Right Measurement

  • Fulfillment
  • Significance
  • Purpose
  • Impact

"Don't measure the plan for your life by acquisitions. Measure it by fulfillment and significance."

The Power of Discipline

"Everyone who competes for the prize is temperate in all things... I discipline my body and bring it into subjection."

1 Corinthians 9:25-27 (NIV)

  • Discipline is about routines
  • Routines sustain life
  • The universe follows routines
  • Our bodies follow routines

Paul's Two Routines

Routine 1: Repeated Thoughts

"I must become, I must win. I must become, I must win."

Routine 2: Repeated Actions

"I keep running. I keep fighting. I don't quit."

"It's so easy to complain without becoming."

Overcoming Life's Unfairness

"The core of the Bible is that no matter what was imposed on you by life, you can choose differently."

  • You didn't choose your mother ➜ but you can choose your lifestyle
  • You didn't choose your village ➜ but you can choose your values
  • You didn't choose your poverty ➜ but you can choose your pursuit
  • You didn't choose your family's history ➜ but you can choose your faith

Mind Before Hand

"The greatest change that must happen will not happen in your hands. It happens in your head before it comes into your hand."

Transformation Sequence

MINDSET ➜ BEING ➜ DOING ➜ HAVING

"If you have a poverty mindset, no matter how much money you are thrown into, you still come out poor."

The "I Have Made It" Trap

"If you don't have this mindset of 'I am free but I choose to be a slave,' there comes a time when you think 'I have made it.' And when you come to 'I have made it,' you stop learning."

The Antidote

  • The educated who learn as if they know nothing
  • The rich who spend wisely as one who has nothing
  • The top performer who trains like a beginner

Action Steps

Mindset Assessment

  • Identify your current destructive slaveries
  • Choose your constructive slaveries
  • Adopt the "I must become" mentality

Routine Development

  • Create your thought loop: "I must become, I must win"
  • Create your action loop: "I keep running, I keep fighting"

Achievement Reframe

  • Shift from acquisitions to significance
  • Ask: "Who do I need to become?" not "What do I want to get?"

Life Is Hard, But Livable

"Life is hard. If anybody tells you the opposite, tell them they haven't lived yet... It's hard but it is livable. We can overcome."

  • Don't accept sympathy as your destiny
  • Don't let people make you settle
  • "I have become. I have made myself. Because I want to win."

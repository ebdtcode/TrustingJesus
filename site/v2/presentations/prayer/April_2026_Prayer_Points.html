Wednesday, April 1

Praying for Souls & HIM-U

Prayer & Intercession

Every Place Where You Set Your Foot

"I will give you every place where you set your foot, as I promised Moses."

Joshua 1:3

  • Thank God for giving Harvest Ministries International every continent, nation, city, community, and neighborhood where Harvesters are residents
  • Pray that more missionaries will come out of Harvest churches to spread the Gospel of Jesus Christ

Nations as Your Inheritance

"Ask me, and I will make the nations your inheritance, the ends of the earth your possession."

Psalm 2:8

  • Pray that nations which accepted Christ before but went cold will be refired
  • Pray that nations that have yet to hear about Jesus will hear the Gospel

Authority Over the Enemy

"I have given you authority to tread on serpents and scorpions, and over all the power of the enemy."

Luke 10:19

  • Thank God that we are going in His Power and Authority and not in our own
  • Declare that all the power of the enemy is rendered powerless as we step in those territories

Empowered to Make Disciples

"All authority in heaven and on earth has been given to me. Therefore go and make disciples of all nations…"

Matthew 28:18-19

  • Declare: I am empowered by the Lord Jesus to make disciples
  • Pray that no culture, way of thinking, or habits will prevent Harvesters from making disciples

Divine Weapons to Demolish Strongholds

"The weapons we fight with are not the weapons of the world. On the contrary, they have divine power to demolish strongholds."

2 Corinthians 10:4

  • Pray that Harvesters are becoming more skilled in using the weapons the Lord gave us to deliver the message of salvation to our world
  • Pray that in the name of Jesus, we are demolishing strongholds that keep unbelievers — coworkers, neighbors, acquaintances — captive

The Kingdom of Our Lord

"The kingdom of the world has become the kingdom of our Lord and of his Messiah, and he will reign for ever and ever."

Revelation 11:15

  • Declare that the DMV and Olney will become the Kingdom of our Lord Jesus
  • Pray that the inhabitants of the DMV and Olney in particular will come to the saving knowledge of Jesus Christ and will submit to His reign

Light of the Gospel

"The god of this age has blinded the minds of unbelievers, so that they cannot see the light of the gospel that displays the glory of Christ, who is the image of God."

2 Corinthians 4:4

  • Pray that the light of the Gospel will shine on the minds of unbelievers so that they are no longer blind but can see the light of the Gospel that displays the glory of Christ, who is the image of God

The ELI Generation

"Go into the village ahead of you; there, as you enter, you will find a colt tied, on which no one has ever sat. Untie it and bring it here."

Luke 19:30

  • Pray that the ELI generation realize that they are of great use to God
  • Pray for the ELI generation and those who are yet to serve that they will yield to the call of God to serve Him in the local church
  • Pray that the ELI generation will accept the discipline and preparation that comes with serving God
Thursday, April 2

Praying for the Easter Service

Prayer & Preparation

The Call of Jesus

"Now on the last and most important day of the feast, Jesus stood and called out [in a loud voice], ‘If anyone is thirsty, let him come to Me and drink!’"

John 7:37

  • Pray that in this Easter season, unbelievers will hear the loud call of Jesus inviting them to receive salvation
  • Pray that unbelievers will respond to the call in large numbers in the local church and globally

Prepare with Excellence

"Jesus sent Peter and John ahead and said, ‘Go and prepare the Passover meal, so we can eat it together.’"

Luke 22:8

  • Pray that Harvesters will work in the spirit of excellence in preparing for Easter Service
  • Pray that each Harvester will come with his/her "ONE" person for Easter service

Consecration & Unity

"Take your place in the sanctuary — a team of Levites for every grouping of your fellow citizens, the laity. Your job is to kill the Passover lambs, then consecrate yourselves and prepare the lambs so that everyone will be able to keep the Passover exactly as God commanded through Moses."

2 Chronicles 35:6

  • Pray that Harvesters will consecrate themselves in this Easter season and do away with anything that does not glorify God in their lives
  • Pray for Harvesters to forgive each other and that relationships be restored so we can come as a united family to celebrate Easter

The Living Word

"For the Word of God is living and powerful, and sharper than any two-edged sword, piercing even to the division of soul and spirit, and of joints and marrow, and is a discerner of the thoughts and intents of the heart."

Hebrews 4:12

  • Pray that the Word preached during Easter will pierce the hearts of the listeners and bring them to repentance and restore them to relationship with Christ

See the Glory of God

"Then Jesus said, ‘Did I not tell you that if you believe, you will see the glory of God?’"

John 11:40

  • Pray that as the Word is preached and believed during Easter Service, miracles, signs and wonders will be in full manifestation

All Eyes on Jesus

"The crowds that went ahead of him and those that followed shouted, ‘Hosanna to the Son of David!’ ‘Blessed is he who comes in the name of the Lord!’ ‘Hosanna in the highest heaven!’"

Matthew 21:9

  • Pray that during the Easter Service all eyes will be on Jesus, and all glory will be given to Him alone

Repentance & Return

"The time promised by God has come at last!" he announced. "The Kingdom of God is near! Repent of your sins and believe the Good News!"

Mark 1:15

  • Pray for the spirit of repentance and forgiveness to sweep through Harvest churches
  • Pray for the backsliders to return to their first love

Serve with Your Gifts

"Each of you should use whatever gift you have received to serve others, as faithful stewards of God's grace in its various forms. If anyone speaks, they should do so as one who speaks the very words of God. If anyone serves, they should do so with the strength God provides, so that in all things God may be praised through Jesus Christ."

1 Peter 4:10-11

  • Thank God for those who already serve and those who will begin to serve at the two services, using whatever gift they have received to serve others
  • Pray for smooth working of the logistics — sound, media, etc. — and for all those who work so that others can simply come and listen

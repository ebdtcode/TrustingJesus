Wednesday, March 4 • Thursday, March 5

Wednesday, March 4

For the Nations, for HIMU

Prayer & Intercession

Pray in the Spirit & Praise

"And everyone present was filled with the Holy Spirit and began speaking in other languages, as the Holy Spirit gave them this ability."

Acts 2:4

  • Spend a few minutes praying in the Spirit

"But you are a chosen people, a royal priesthood, a holy nation, God's special possession, that you may declare the praises of Him who called you out of darkness into His wonderful light."

1 Peter 2:9

  • Praise the Lord: Spend a few minutes doing what He called us to do

Thanksgiving for Answered Prayer

"This is the confidence we have in approaching God: that if we ask anything according to His will, He hears us. And if we know that He hears us — whatever we ask — we know that we have what we asked of Him."

1 John 5:14-15

  • Thank God for answering our prayers for the Harvest International Conference
  • Thank Him that He took attendees safely to and from the conference — most of Olney's pastors and pastors from all of HIMU were present in one place, but God kept His people safe

God's Work in HIMU

"And I am certain that God, who began the good work within you, will continue his work until it is finally finished on the day when Christ Jesus returns."

Philippians 1:6

  • Thank God for the magnificent work He is doing in HIMU — how He has brought us from many nations and made us one that we might win the lost no matter the cost
  • Declare that He who began it will accomplish it

Workers for the Harvest

"Then he said to his disciples, 'The harvest is plentiful but the workers are few. Ask the Lord of the harvest, therefore, to send out workers into his harvest field.'"

Matthew 9:37-38

  • Pray to the Lord of the Harvest to send out workers into the fields to those who may be ready and waiting to receive the Gospel
  • Pray that more and more of HIMU's members will go out into the fields

Draw Hearts to Jesus

"No one can come to me unless the Father who sent me draws them, and I will raise them up at the last day."

John 6:44

"Satan, who is the god of this world, has blinded the minds of those who don't believe. They are unable to see the glorious light of the Good News."

2 Corinthians 4:4

  • Pray that as workers go into the harvest fields, the Father will draw the hearts of the people to Jesus
  • Pray that God, who brought light from darkness, will shine the glorious light of the Gospel in the hearts of those who are perishing

Wisdom for Leaders

"If any of you lacks wisdom, you should ask God, who gives generously to all without finding fault, and it will be given to you."

James 1:5 (NIV)

  • Thank God for the wisdom He generously provides to all without finding fault
  • Pray for the Presiding Prelate (Bishop Johnson) and all the other Bishops, that God give them wisdom as they lead HIMU
  • Pray for God's continuous protection, provision, and leading upon all the HIMU Pastors and their wives, and all missionaries

Pray for the Nations

"I urge you, first of all, to pray for all people. Ask God to help them; intercede on their behalf, and give thanks for them. Pray this way for kings and all who are in authority so that we can live peaceful and quiet lives marked by godliness and dignity."

1 Timothy 2:1-2 (NLT)

  • Pray for the nations — where there is war, He will bring peace; where there is famine, He will bring relief; where there is genocide, He will bring an end
  • Pray that God will touch the hearts of those persecuting the church, that they may come to know Him as Saul did on the road to Damascus

Resources & Protection for HIMU

"And God is able to bless you abundantly, so that in all things at all times, having all that you need, you will abound in every good work."

2 Corinthians 9:8 (NIV)

  • Pray for an abundance of resources for every good work the Lord has called HIMU to do
  • Pray that HIMU members will be faithful to give generously to HIMU projects

"No weapon formed against you shall prosper, and every tongue which rises against you in judgment you shall condemn."

Isaiah 54:17 (NKJV)

  • Declare no weapon formed against HIMU, the Bishops, Pastors, and members shall prosper
  • Declare that HIMU goes from faith to faith, from strength to strength, and from glory to glory through Christ Jesus

Secrets Brought to Light

"For all that is secret will eventually be brought into the open, and everything that is concealed will be brought to light and made known to all."

Luke 8:17

  • Pray that the things done in secret against this country will be brought to light
  • Pray that God will "bring to nothing" the plans, counsel, and evil strategies of those working against the nation, and that those involved will be exposed and brought to judgment
Thursday, March 5

For Harvest Olney

Prayer & Declarations

Opening Declarations

Declare

God not only hears our prayers, but He answers. Isaiah 65:24

Declare

Our God is able to do exceedingly abundantly above all that we ask or think according to the power that works in us. Ephesians 3:20

Declare

All things work together for our good. We are more than conquerors. Nothing can separate us from the love of God. Romans 8:28, 37, 39

God's Word Accomplishes

"The rain and snow come down from the heavens and stay on the ground to water the earth. They cause the grain to grow, producing seed for the farmer and bread for the hungry. It is the same with my word. I send it out, and it always produces fruit. It will accomplish all I want it to, and it will prosper everywhere I send it."

Isaiah 55:10-11 (NLT)

  • Declare that what the Lord has said of us at Harvest Olney as a church, and as individuals, none shall return to Him empty, but shall not only accomplish what the Lord pleases, but it shall prosper

God Works While We Wait

"For since the world began, no ear has heard and no eye has seen a God like You, who works for those who wait for Him!"

Isaiah 64:4 (NLT)

  • Declare that our God is working as we wait for answers to our prayers
  • Pray 2 Corinthians 4:18 — that as we wait, the Holy Spirit will help us to "fix our eyes not on what is seen, but on what is unseen, since what is seen is temporary, but what is unseen is eternal"

Workers for the Kingdom

"Work willingly at whatever you do, as though you were working for the Lord rather than for people. Remember that the Lord will give you an inheritance as your reward, and that the Master you are serving is Christ."

Colossians 3:23-24

  • Thank God for the workers we have now — workers who faithfully served while most pastors were at the International Conference for over 12 days
  • Pray that God will raise up more workers as we move to two services
  • Pray that we will all work willingly as though working for the Lord, remembering the Master we serve is Christ
  • Thank God that He rewards those who work for Him

Easter & the Power of the Spirit

"But God demonstrates his own love for us in this: While we were still sinners, Christ died for us."

Romans 5:8

  • Thank God for this demonstration of His love — that Jesus died while we were yet sinners, and that He arose! Thank God for Resurrection Sunday!

"After they prayed, the place where they were meeting was shaken. And they were all filled with the Holy Spirit and spoke the word of God boldly."

Acts 4:31 (NIV)

  • Pray that at our Easter services, the heavens will be open over us in the power and in the manifestations of the Holy Spirit and His gifts

Jehovah Jireh, Our Provider

"And this same God who takes care of me will supply all your needs from His glorious riches, which have been given to us in Christ Jesus."

Philippians 4:19

  • Declare that God our Father is Jehovah Jireh, our Provider
  • Declare that He will provide all the finances for Invest Beyond

"Give, and you will receive. Your gift will return to you in full — pressed down, shaken together to make room for more, running over, and poured into your lap."

Luke 6:38

  • Pray that the Holy Spirit will make this Word real in our lives, for those who give and for those still struggling to give to the Lord's work

God's Presence in Every Service

"For where two or three gather together as my followers, I am there among them."

Matthew 18:20

  • Pray that this fact becomes our experience at each service: a manifestation of God's Presence in our midst
  • Declare that the Glory of the Lord is present at Harvest Olney and fills every service
  • Declare that people are being born again and baptized in the Holy Spirit every week
  • Declare that no one comes and leaves in the same way because God is in our midst

Closing: He Hears & Answers

"This is the confidence we have in approaching God: that if we ask anything according to His will, He hears us. And if we know that He hears us — whatever we ask — we know that we have what we asked of Him."

1 John 5:14-15

Thank God

Thank God for hearing and answering our prayers!

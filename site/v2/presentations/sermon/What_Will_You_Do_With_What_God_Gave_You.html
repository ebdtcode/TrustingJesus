Sermon

What Will You Do With What God Gave You?

Bishop Darlingston Johnson

Bethel World Outreach Church | March 05, 2026

Entrusted With the Gospel

"But as we have been approved by God to be entrusted with the gospel, even so we speak, not as pleasing men, but God who tests our hearts."

— 1 Thessalonians 2:4

  • God has entrusted us with the gospel.
  • Faithfulness to this call is essential.
  • We must prioritize sharing the message.

Prioritize the Message

  • The gospel must be first in our lives.
  • It is the power of God for salvation.
  • Do not delay or minimize its importance.

The gospel is the power of God unto salvation.

Faithfulness With Resources

"For the kingdom of heaven is like a man traveling to a far country, who called his own servants and delivered his goods to them."

— Matthew 25:14

  • God entrusts us with time, talents, and treasure.
  • Faithful stewardship leads to eternal rewards.
  • Unfaithfulness results in loss.

Personalize the Mission

  • The Great Commission is for every believer.
  • Take personal responsibility for sharing the gospel.
  • Start where you are—your mission field is local.

This responsibility is your responsibility.

Prayer for Faithfulness

  • Ask God for a heart that prioritizes the gospel.
  • Pray for courage to act immediately in obedience.
  • Seek guidance to use resources for His kingdom.

Participate Fully

  • God calls us to full-time faithfulness.
  • Use your resources daily for the kingdom.
  • Do not serve sporadically or out of convenience.

God isn't looking for part-time missionaries.

Work While It Is Day

"I must work the works of Him who sent Me while it is day; the night is coming when no one can work."

— John 9:4

  • Time is limited—act now.
  • The harvest is ripe every day.
  • Be diligent in kingdom work.

Two Types of Believers

  • Active believers invest in the kingdom.
  • Passive believers bury their resources.
  • Procrastination is disobedience in slow motion.

Be an active believer, not a passive one.

Prayer for Obedience

  • Pray for deliverance from procrastination.
  • Ask for boldness to use your gifts for God.
  • Seek wisdom to prioritize kingdom work.

Eternal Perspective

"His lord said to him, ‘Well done, good and faithful servant; you were faithful over a few things, I will make you ruler over many things. Enter into the joy of your lord.’"

— Matthew 25:23

  • Faithfulness leads to eternal rewards.
  • Live daily with accountability to Jesus.
  • Strive for His commendation: 'Well done.'

God's Measure of Faithfulness

  • God values what we do with what we have.
  • Use resources to proclaim the gospel.
  • Faithfulness matters more than quantity.

Faithfulness is measured by obedience, not abundance.

Living It Out

  • Reflect on how you prioritize the gospel daily.
  • Identify ways to participate in the Great Commission.
  • Start using your resources immediately for God.

Final Challenge

  • Evaluate your stewardship before God.
  • Commit to faithfulness today.
  • Live for eternal approval, not earthly applause.

Let there be no regrets—live for His glory.

Closing / Call to Respond

"His lord said to him, ‘Well done, good and faithful servant.’"

— Matthew 25:21

Commit to faithfulness today. Use your resources for His kingdom.

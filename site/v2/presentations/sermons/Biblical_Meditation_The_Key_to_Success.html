How to Meditate

Practical Steps for Transformation

The Meditation Process

  • Read/hear the Word - Allow truth to enter your mind
  • Focus your attention - Choose a specific truth or promise
  • Repeat, review, rehearse - Go over it multiple times
  • Speak it, declare it - Use your mouth to proclaim it
  • Keep it before your eyes - Use imagination to visualize
  • See yourself living it - Imagine walking in that reality
  • Exchange your thoughts - Continue until transformation occurs
  • Practice day and night - Make it a habit, not occasional

Stop Dipping, Start Soaking

The Sponge

  • Don't just dip and take out

  • Let the Word permeate your entire mind

The Tea Bag

  • Quick dip doesn't make strong tea

  • Keep your mind in God's Word

The Thermostat

Prayer for Commitment & Transformation

Committing to Daily Meditation

Prayer for Victory & Grace

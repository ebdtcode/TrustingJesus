The Peace Child Story

A Cultural Bridge to the Gospel

The Missionary's Dilemma

  • Missionaries to the Sawi people of South America
  • Gospel message was incomprehensible to them
  • In their culture, betrayal was considered a virtue
  • Judas, not Jesus, was seen as the hero
  • How do you share the gospel when the betrayer is celebrated?

God's Wisdom Revealed

  • Two warring villages decided to make peace
  • One king gave his son to the other tribe
  • This child was called the "Peace Child"
  • As long as the Peace Child lived, there could be no war
  • The exchange made the two tribes one people

The Revelation

"Tell them Jesus is the Peace Child!"
God opened the missionary's eyes to see that Jesus Christ is God's Peace Child - the divine solution to the war between God and humanity.

Jesus: The Ultimate Peace Child

  • War existed between God and man due to sin
  • This war had lasted for thousands of years
  • God provided His own Son as the Peace Child
  • As long as Jesus lives, God is at peace with believers
  • Accepting the Peace Child ends the war forever

The Heart of the Gospel

"Jesus is the Peace Child"
He is not only the one who makes peace - He Himself IS our peace with God.

The War Has Ended

Understanding Our New Reality

A Powerful Metaphor

"The bombs have stopped falling"
The war that once existed between God and man has ceased. The enmity is over. The hostility has ended.

Your New Reality in Christ

  • No war exists between you and God
  • God is completely pleased with you
  • He has nothing against you in His heart
  • All charges have been dropped completely
  • God is not fighting against you
  • He is not withholding His love or favor

God's Attitude in Christ

In Christ, God is:
  • Not offended by your sins
  • Not withholding His love
  • Not withholding Himself
  • Not planning to punish you
  • Completely at peace with you

God Is FOR You

Romans 8:31-39

What Then Shall We Say?

"What then shall we say to these things? If God is for us, who can be against us?"

Romans 8:31

The Ultimate Proof

"He who did not spare His own Son, but delivered Him up for us all, how shall He not with Him also freely give us all things?"

Romans 8:32

Secure from All Accusations

"Who shall bring a charge against God's elect? It is God who justifies. Who is he who condemns? It is Christ who died, and furthermore is also risen, who is even at the right hand of God, who also makes intercession for us."

Romans 8:33-34

Eternally Secure

"For I am persuaded that neither death nor life, nor angels nor principalities nor powers, nor things present nor things to come, nor height nor depth, nor any other created thing, shall be able to separate us from the love of God which is in Christ Jesus our Lord."

Romans 8:38-39

The Legal Foundation

Not Emotional, But Legal

Why the Legal Foundation Matters

  • No one respects the law more than God
  • God's love never gets in the way of His law
  • Everything God does must be done legally
  • He had to follow the law to the letter
  • The law is the expression of God's holiness

What the Law Demands

  • "The soul that sins shall die"
  • "Without the shedding of blood, no remission"
  • God has never forgiven anyone apart from blood
  • It would be illegal for God to ignore these requirements
  • Christ fulfilled every requirement perfectly

You Have a Legal Deed

"This peace is mine legally"
Just as a property deed gives you legal ownership, Christ's work gives you legal standing before God. When challenged, you can present your deed - God's Word!

Jesus Met Every Requirement

  • Lived a perfectly sinless life
  • Loved God with all His heart, mind, soul, strength
  • Fulfilled the law completely
  • Never once broke any commandment
  • Qualified as the spotless Lamb of God

The Heavenly Court Scene

  • God the Judge examines the evidence
  • Jesus our Defense Lawyer presents His case
  • He shows His broken body and shed blood
  • God examines Christ's perfect life
  • The law's requirements are fully satisfied
  • Verdict: "NOT GUILTY!"

The Prodigal Son

A Picture of Salvation

The Son's Departure

  • "I don't want to be under your authority"
  • "I don't want to listen to you"
  • "I want to make my own decisions"
  • "Give me my inheritance and let me go"
  • He spent everything on sinful living

The Awakening

  • He lost everything through sinful choices
  • The wages of sin brought death and despair
  • He "came to his senses"
  • "Even the servants have enough to eat"
  • He decided to return home

Amazing Grace in Action

  • The father was watching the road every day
  • He saw his son "while he was yet far off"
  • The father RAN to meet him
  • He laid aside all dignity to reach his son
  • Grace reached him before law could judge him

Treated as If He Never Left

"God treats us as though we never left home"
  • The best robe was put on him
  • A ring was placed on his finger
  • Shoes were put on his feet
  • The fatted calf was killed
  • A celebration was thrown

How God Sees You

God treats you exactly as He would treat Jesus:
  • As though you never left home
  • As though you never sinned
  • As though you never rebelled
  • As a faithful, loving child
  • With complete acceptance and joy

Access to Grace

Standing in Transformation

Not only do we have peace with God, but we have ongoing access to His transforming grace.

  • You are joined to Christ who is grace
  • Through union with Him, you bear His fruit
  • Grace breaks the power of sin over you
  • You become a new creation in Christ

Internal Transformation

  • Your desires begin to change
  • You develop new appetites
  • Sin loses its appeal
  • Holiness becomes attractive
  • Transformation happens from the inside out

Divine Partnership

Philippians 2:13

God doesn't just save you and leave you - He continues to work in you, changing your will and empowering your actions.

Your New Identity

2 Corinthians 5:17

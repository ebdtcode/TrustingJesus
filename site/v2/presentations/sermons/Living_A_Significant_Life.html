Three Levels of Living

  • Survival - Characterized by scarcity, just getting by day to day
  • Success - The level of abundance, multiple options, material prosperity
  • Significance - Life transcends self to serve others and bring glory to God
"Jesus did not come so we could merely survive, but that we might have life abundantly"

Significance

The Highest Level of Living

The Heart of Significance

Loving God

  • With all your heart
  • With all your strength
  • With all your mind
  • This is worship

Loving Your Neighbor

  • As yourself
  • This is ministry
  • Serving others
  • Bringing glory to God

"Love the Lord your God with all your heart, strength, and mind, and love your neighbor as yourself"

Matthew 22:37-39

Four Principles

For Living A Significant Life

1. Acknowledge the Grace of God

  • Peter was discouraged - fished all night, caught nothing
  • Jesus chose Peter's boat out of two available boats
  • Grace located Peter when he was at his lowest point
  • Everything we are and have is by grace, not our own strength

"By the grace of God I am what I am"

1 Corinthians 15:10

2. Surrender Your Boats

  • Peter allowed Jesus to use his boat to minister to crowds
  • The boat represented his investment, business, asset, treasure
  • What are your "boats"? Gifts, talents, time, money, career?
  • Put kingdom needs first, even when personal needs seem pressing
"Like the widow who gave her mite, we must release what we have, no matter how small or desperate our circumstances"

3. What You Surrender is Empowered

  • The same boat and nets that caught nothing all night
  • Became supernaturally productive when surrendered to Jesus
  • They caught so many fish the nets began to tear
  • Both boats began to sink from the abundance

God Multiplies What We Release

What seems useless in our hands becomes fruitful in His

The Miraculous Catch

"Master, we have toiled all night and caught nothing; nevertheless at Your word I will let down the net. And when they had done this, they caught a great number of fish, and their net was breaking."

Luke 5:5-6

"Nevertheless at Your word" - Obedience despite disappointment leads to supernatural breakthrough

4. Beyond the Boat, Surrender Your Life

  • Peter started by surrendering his boat
  • After the miracle, he fell at Jesus' feet recognizing his sinfulness
  • He ultimately left everything to follow Christ
  • We cannot truly love God without loving Him with our resources

"Where your treasure is, there your heart will also be"

Matthew 6:21

Prayer Focus

Transition from Survival to Significance

"Lord, lift me from merely surviving to living a life of eternal significance. Transform my seasons of disappointment into demonstrations of Your glory. Help me embrace the call to love You with all my heart and to love my neighbor as myself. Let my life be marked by how I glorify You and bless others."

Based on John 10:10; Matthew 22:37-39

Prayer Focus

Surrendering Our Resources

"Lord Jesus, like Peter who surrendered his boat when struggling, I release my gifts, talents, time, and resources into Your hands. Help me put Your kingdom first. Enable me to give sacrificially, trusting that what I surrender will be multiplied for Your glory."

Based on Matthew 6:33; Luke 5:1-11

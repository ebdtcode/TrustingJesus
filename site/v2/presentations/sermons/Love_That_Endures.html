Love That Endures

Pastor Peabody

1 Corinthians 13:4 • John 5:24-26 • Ephesians 3:14-19

You Have Passed from Death to Life

"Most assuredly, I say to you, he who hears My word and believes in Him who sent Me has everlasting life, and shall not come into judgment, but has passed from death into life."

John 5:24 (NKJV)

Key Insight

You already have eternal life. You're not waiting to die to receive it. You have zoe life right now.

Zoe Life: God's Life Within You

  • The Father has life in Himself and granted the Son to have life in Himself
  • If you have the Son, you have this life (1 John 5:12)
  • Your dead situations can hear the voice of the Son of God from within you
  • Speak to your circumstances — let zoe speak through you

Zoe Enables Supernatural Love

It is this zoe life that enables us to love the way God loves.

The Challenge

The way the world is going, it's easy to hate. But we have a backup — we have zoe life. We must become aware that this life is there, so we can lean on it.

Exercise Your Spiritual Muscles

"If I go to the gym, I will have a six pack. But right now I've got one pack. I don't exercise the muscles. It's possible to have this life and not use it."

Jesus told His disciples: "The spirit is willing, but the flesh is weak." It is possible for a believer not to be strong in the Lord.

Two Prongs of Faith

1. Believing Faith

Acquiring knowledge from God's Word. If your knowledge is weak, your faith will be weak. Get into the Word for yourself.

2. Speaking Faith

Declaring what God has said. Even when you can't see it, trust God and speak His Word over your situation.

Stop Living in the Lobby

When Annie got into Daddy Warbucks' house, she ran upstairs, went into every room, discovering everything that was her father's.

But Many Believers...

Come into the Kingdom of God and stand right there in the lobby for 10, 20 years. Jesus died to give you all of this — explore your inheritance!

Core Teaching

What Love Is

"Love suffers long and is kind..."

1 Corinthians 13:4 (NKJV)

Love suffers long AND is kind.

Kind While Suffering

  • Love is not only long-suffering — love is kind while suffering
  • Kind under duress. Kind under pressure.
  • Love does not merely endure suffering — it maintains its posture whilst under pressure
  • It guards your heart — "this will not get under my skin"

The Danger: Endurance Without Kindness

Many can remain, but...

  • They grow cold — that job you used to love, now Monday morning makes you mad
  • They become bitter — still in the relationship but seething inside
  • They become callous — two people under the same roof, but the relationship has gone cold

The Result

Endurance without kindness = Resentment

Kindness Without Patience

The other imbalance

Someone who doesn't usually care about you suddenly comes smiling and doing good things. Something's wrong. You know they want something. Like your children saying, "Daddy, can I wash the dishes?"

The Result

Kindness without patience = Temporary & Manipulative

Biblical Love Holds Both

Patience

Long-suffering

+
Kindness

Warmth & tenderness

It bears all things and consciously remains warm. For the joy set before me, I will endure.

Blessing While Bleeding

"And Jesus said to him, 'Assuredly, I say to you, today you will be with Me in Paradise.'"

Luke 23:43 (NKJV)

  • Jesus did not just endure the cross — He blessed while bleeding
  • No membership class needed. "Today you shall be with Me in paradise."
  • He forgave while suffering: "Father, forgive them"
  • He served while dying: "Woman, behold your son"

A Soft Answer Saves a Life

Testimony from a Sierra Leone prison

"A soft answer turns away wrath, but a harsh word stirs up anger."

Proverbs 15:1 (NKJV)

What Happened

A rebel kicked him from his spot. Every instinct said to fight back. But the scripture came to mind. A simple smile changed everything: "This place is your place. You are my partner from now on."

The Measure of Spiritual Maturity

The proof of spiritual maturity is not how long you can endure, but how kind you remain while suffering. Anyone can survive. Only Christ-formed love can suffer long and remain kind.

Paul's Prayer

Strengthened in the Inner Man

"For this reason I bow my knees to the Father... that He would grant you, according to the riches of His glory, to be strengthened with might through His Spirit in the inner man."

Ephesians 3:14-16 (NKJV)

Hold Fast: Stay on the Bull

If you can stay on for 8 seconds, you're good

  • The bull rider studies the animal, grips the rope, uses one hand
  • The world is trying to knock you off your spot
  • Paul says: "Having done all to stand — STAND"
  • Hold fast the profession of your faith

How?

By exercising your spirit through prayer. A prayerless believer is a weak believer. Come to prayer!

Rooted and Grounded in Love

"That Christ may dwell in your hearts through faith; that you, being rooted and grounded in love, may be able to comprehend with all the saints what is the width and length and depth and height."

Ephesians 3:17-18 (NKJV)

Four Dimensions of God's Love

Breadth

Reaches people not in your sphere, not your color, who don't speak your language

Length

Unending — from eternal past to eternal future, always present

Depth

Reaches trauma, heals the deepest wounds, the sounds and triggers that haunt you

Height

Removes you from earth and seats you far above principalities and powers

Filled With the Fullness of God

"To know the love of Christ which passes knowledge; that you may be filled with all the fullness of God."

Ephesians 3:19 (NKJV)

What God desires for you to accomplish — be filled with it. He has given you what it takes to love the way He does.

Don't Quit!

  • You shall not die — you will live!
  • Your business will not die. Your marriage will not die.
  • You've been in there for 20 years — you're at the gate, ready to receive the reward
  • Guard your heart. Continue to remain tender.
  • Do not get callous. There is no time to quit.
Response

Prayer Points

From the sermon on Enduring Love

Prayer: Activate Zoe Life

Father, we have passed from death to life and possess Your zoe life within us. Make us aware of this supernatural life. Help us exercise it daily and depend on it in every challenging situation. Let us no longer remain in the "lobby" of Christianity but explore the fullness of everything Jesus died to give us.

John 5:24-26; 1 John 5:11-12

Prayer: Suffer Long AND Be Kind

Lord Jesus, forgive us for enduring without kindness, which produces resentment. Strengthen us to bear all things while remaining warm, to forgive while being wounded, to bless while bleeding, and to serve while suffering, just as You did on the cross. Let kindness under duress be our distinguishing mark.

1 Corinthians 13:4, 7; Luke 23:34, 42-43

Prayer: Strengthen the Inner Man

Father, grant us according to the riches of Your glory to be strengthened with might through Your Spirit in our inner man. A prayerless believer is a weak believer. Stir up hunger in us to exercise our spiritual muscles. Help us hold fast and stand firm when the enemy tries to knock us off our position.

Ephesians 3:14-16; Hebrews 10:23; Ephesians 6:13

Prayer: Guard Against Callousness

Lord, protect our hearts from the spirit of callousness, bitterness, and coldness that tries to set in after prolonged suffering. If we have allowed coldness to settle, we repent. Rekindle the fire of Your love within us. Let us remain tender, kind, and warm no matter how long we've been in the battle.

Proverbs 4:23; Hebrews 12:15; Ephesians 4:31-32

Prayer: For Those Ready to Quit

Lord, we lift up every person at the point of quitting. We declare: I shall not die, but live, and declare the works of the Lord! You are not at the beginning — you are at the gate, ready to receive your reward. Don't quit now! The Lord will take you through. Be strengthened with might in your spirit.

Psalm 118:17; Hebrews 10:35-36; Galatians 6:9

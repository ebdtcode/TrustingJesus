Let My Sentence Come Forth

"Hear the right, O Lord, attend unto my cry, give ear unto my prayer, that goeth not out of feigned lips. Let my sentence come forth from thy presence; let thine eyes behold the things that are equal."

Psalm 17:1-2 (KJV)

Your Sentence

Your sentence is not coming from any earthly court — it is coming from the presence of God.

What Is a Sentence?

  • A decree by a set authority based on the law of the land
  • It enters into action the moment it is pronounced
  • When you are sentenced, everyone around you is sentenced too
  • Your family, your business, your future — all affected by the verdict

Today

Your sentence is not coming from a doctor, a bank, or a politician — it is coming from the Court of Heaven.

The Court of Heaven

The Books Are Opened

"As I watched, thrones were set in place, and the Ancient of Days took his seat... The court was seated, and the books were opened."

Daniel 7:9-10

Your life is going to be according to the books opened in the court of heaven — not what the world has said about you.

The Accuser Has Access

"Now there was a day when the sons of God came to present themselves before the Lord, and Satan also came among them."

Job 1:6 (NKJV)

  • The devil has the audacity to enter God's presence and accuse you
  • Before redemption, we were outside the courtroom — dead in trespasses (Colossians 2:13)
  • We could not defend ourselves

The strength of the accuser is your absence.

The Divine Conference

While the accuser accused, the Godhead — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — entered into a divine conference.

The Decision

Yeshua would leave the Godhead, come in the flesh, and go to the cross on our behalf. "Tetelestai" — It is finished.

Water, Blood, New Life

The Water Came Out

From the pierced side of Christ — washing and cleansing

The Blood Came Out

A DNA transfer — the very life of God entering into you

A New Life Was Born

Born from above — you are now part of the family of God with full access to the courtroom

The Evidence

Three Witnesses That Agree

1 John 5:8 (NKJV)

  • The Holy Spirit — "I was there at the cross"
  • The Blood — "I came from His side to pay the price"
  • The Water — "I came forth to wash and make clean"

Every Accusation Wiped Out

Colossians 2:14 (NKJV)

"Having disarmed principalities and powers, He made a public spectacle of them, triumphing over them in it."

Colossians 2:15 (NKJV)

The Verdict

The Judge Has Ruled

"Therefore if the Son makes you free, you shall be free indeed."

John 8:36 (NKJV)

The case is over. It is settled. You are free.

Our Inheritance Is Legal

"And if children, then heirs — heirs of God and joint heirs with Christ."

Romans 8:17 (NKJV)

  • Jesus is legally the heir of all creation (Hebrews 1:2)
  • You are legally a joint heir — an equal heir with Jesus
  • Healing, provision, restoration — these are not favors to beg for
  • They are verdicts already rendered by the court of heaven
Testimony

The Power of the Decree

Papa Billy's twin grandchildren were born at five months — so premature they fit in the palm of a hand. Doctors said to prepare for burial.

  • He took communion with his team and decreed life based on the blood covenant
  • "If it's not written in the blood, it's not my portion"
  • Sent the decree to be played continuously in the ICU
  • Both children fully restored — now four years old and thriving
The Sentence

Full and Complete Restoration

"So the king appointed unto her a certain officer, saying, Restore all that was hers, and all the fruits of the field since the day that she left the land, even until now."

2 Kings 8:6 (KJV)

Not some — ALL. Including the fruit of the field from every year you were away.

Decree

Everything the enemy has swallowed — your health, your wealth, your relationships, your opportunities — he is vomiting it up now in the name of Jesus.

Seven Times Speed

  • What others accomplish in seven years, you accomplish in one
  • Your health is recovering speedily

Response

Prayer Points

From the sermon on My Sentence

Prayer: Silence the Accuser

Father, we present the only answer that silences every accusation: the blood of Jesus Christ. Every handwriting of ordinances that was against us has been wiped out and nailed to the cross. We plead the blood over every accusation, every curse, every legal claim the enemy holds against our families, our health, and our future. The three witnesses agree — the case is dismissed.

Colossians 2:14; 1 John 5:7-8; Revelation 12:11

Prayer: Receive Our Legal Inheritance

Heavenly Father, we are joint heirs with Christ — equally heirs. Our healing is legally ours. Our provision is legally ours. We come not as beggars pleading for a favor, but as heirs presenting our title deed. We present the blood as evidence, the water as witness, and the Holy Spirit as advocate. We take legal possession of every blessing that belongs to us in Christ.

Hebrews 1:2; Romans 8:17; Colossians 2:15; John 8:36

Prayer: Full and Complete Restoration

Lord, as it was decreed in 2 Kings 8:6 — "Restore ALL that was hers" — we receive that decree today. Full restoration of health, finances, marriages, children, businesses, calling, and passion for prayer. What the enemy has swallowed, he shall vomit up. Every resource stolen, every year consumed, every opportunity hijacked — we call it back by the authority of the court of heaven.

2 Kings 8:6; Job 20:15; Joel 2:25-26

Prayer: Seven Times Speed

Father, we receive the decree of seven times speed over our lives. What others accomplish in seven years, we accomplish in one. We reject every spirit of delay and stagnation. The God who said "Tetelestai" has completed the work for our acceleration. The sentence is not pending — it entered into action the moment it was pronounced. We walk in it now.

2 Kings 8:6; Isaiah 60:22; John 19:30; Psalm 17:2

Prayer: For Our Families

Father, we stand as intercessors for our families. For every loved one under attack — a diagnosis, a debt, a broken relationship — we appeal to the highest court in the universe. As Papa Billy decreed life over his grandchildren from across the world, we decree life, health, salvation, and restoration now. The case is in the hands of the Judge of all the earth, and He has already ruled in our favor through the blood of His Son.

Acts 16:31; 1 John 5:7-8; Psalm 17:1-2

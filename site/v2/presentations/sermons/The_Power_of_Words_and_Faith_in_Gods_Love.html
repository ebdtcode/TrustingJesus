The Power of Words and Faith in God's Love

"Death and life are in the power of the tongue, and those who love it will eat its fruit."

Proverbs 18:21

Bishop Dr. Darlingston Johnson
Harvest Intercontinental Church - Olney
January 11, 2026

God's Love Requires Our Agreement

  • God's love is almighty and can do anything
  • The question is not "Does God love me?" - that's a fact
  • The question is: Am I agreeing with His love?
  • Agreement is expressed through our words and works
  • Your experience is affected by the choices you make
"God's love opens the door, but you have a choice to walk through it or not"

Words Release Authority

The Creative Power of Speech

God Creates by Speaking

"And God said, 'Let there be light,' and there was light."

Genesis 1:3

  • Every star, every planet - created by speaking
  • God didn't have to move - He just had to speak
  • All things were framed by the word of God (Hebrews 11:3)
  • His Word continues to hold everything together

The Woman with the Issue of Blood

  • 12 years of suffering - doctors couldn't help
  • She heard about Jesus - faith comes by hearing
  • She kept saying: "If I just touch His garment, I will be healed"
  • Her words and works came together in alignment with who Jesus was
  • Jesus said: "Your faith has made you whole"
"She began to speak the healing out of her mouth"

The Roman Centurion

  • His servant was sick unto death - hopeless situation
  • He understood authority and how it works
  • Said to Jesus: "You don't have to come, just speak the word"
  • Jesus marveled at his faith
  • Words carry authority and command power

"Just speak the word, and my servant will be healed... I also am a man under authority."

Matthew 8:8-9

Created in God's Image

Dominion Through Speech

Man Created to Govern with Words

Why Only Man Can Speak

  • Animals have greater senses
  • But only man can speak
  • Speech is for the governor
  • Authority is exercised through words

Adam Named the Animals

  • God brought animals to Adam
  • Whatever Adam said, that was its name
  • God respected Adam's authority
  • Adam governed through his words

"Let them have dominion over the fish of the sea, over the birds of the air, and over every living thing..."

Genesis 1:26

Words Create, Not Just Describe

  • God doesn't use words to describe reality
  • God uses words to create reality
  • If you keep describing your problems, you reinforce them
  • Use your words to create the reality you want to see
  • Speak to your mountain and command it to move

"Whoever says to this mountain, 'Be removed and be cast into the sea,' and does not doubt in his heart... he will have whatever he says."

Mark 11:23

Life and Death in the Tongue

"Death and life are in the power of the tongue, and those who love it will eat its fruit."

Proverbs 18:21

Words of Life Release:

  • Faith
  • Hope
  • Love

Words of Death Release:

  • Fear
  • Hopelessness
  • Hatred
"With our words we create the conditions that allow the spirit of life or the spirit of death to work"

Speak Blessing, Inherit Blessing

"Not returning evil for evil or reviling for reviling, but on the contrary blessing, knowing that you were called to this, that you may inherit a blessing."

1 Peter 3:9

  • Don't curse back when cursed - bless instead
  • Your words impact whether you possess your blessings
  • Many have unclaimed blessings because of careless speech
  • When you let emotions control your speech, you block your inheritance

Bridle Your Tongue

"If anyone among you thinks he is religious, and does not bridle his tongue but deceives his own heart, this one's religion is useless."

James 1:26

  • You can come to church, pay tithe, do good works...
  • But if you don't bridle your tongue, it's worthless
  • No matter how much we fast and pray - without controlling speech, it's useless
  • Don't let feelings control your speech

The Tongue Steers Your Life

  • The tongue is like a rudder that turns the ship
  • A small rudder turns a massive ship
  • Your words set the direction of your life
  • Keep the rudder where you want to go
  • Don't let circumstances change your confession

"Look also at ships: although they are so large and are driven by fierce winds, they are turned by a very small rudder..."

James 3:4

Choose Life

With Your Words

Choose Life Through Your Words

"I call heaven and earth as witnesses today against you, that I have set before you life and death, blessing and cursing; therefore choose life..."

Deuteronomy 30:19

  • Speak what God says, not what circumstances say
  • My God supplies all my needs according to His riches
  • By His stripes I am healed
  • I will live and not die
  • Spend time speaking the reality you're believing for

Prayer Focus

For a Transformed Tongue

"Father, I ask for a complete transformation of my speech patterns. Help me bridle my tongue and speak only words that align with Your Word and character. Remove from me all complaining, murmuring, and negative declarations that contradict Your promises. Let every word I speak release faith, hope, and love."

Based on Psalm 19:14, James 1:26

Prayer Focus

For Faith-Filled Declarations

"Lord Jesus, You taught that if we speak to the mountain with faith and do not doubt in our hearts, whatever we say will come to pass. I declare Your Word over every impossible situation in my life. I will not merely describe my problems; I will speak directly to them with the authority You have given me in Your name."

Based on Mark 11:23

Choose Words of Life

Align Your Speech with God's Word

  • Your words carry authority and command power
  • Speak to create reality, not just describe it
  • Bridle your tongue - control your future
  • Every time you speak God's Word, you exercise spiritual authority

Declaration

I choose life! I choose to speak words of life over myself, my family, and my circumstances. I align my words with God's Word and His character in Jesus' name!
